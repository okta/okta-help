What you can expect

The upgrade consists of a few steps that are managed by Okta:

  • Okta provides support documentation.

  • In most cases, you can upgrade your org’s preview environments first and test the changes with users.

  • You can make slight modifications to prepare your org for upgrade. This typically means turning off deprecated or unsupported features in .

  • After the upgrade, Okta provides a list of best practices on how to test your org.

  • Your existing features and products are carried over during the upgrade process.
