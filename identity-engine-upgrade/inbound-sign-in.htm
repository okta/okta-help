Federated inbound sign-in flow

Learn how the sign-in flow changes for inbound federation or -to- SSO.

Change summary The Sign-In Widget reappears for end users during an inbound federation or Okta-to-Okta SSO flow. In Classic Engine, redisplay of the Sign-In Widget was suppressed.
Admin experience n/a
User experience After a user signs in from a Service Provider or from another org, a portion of the sign-in page reappears during the transition to the target application.
