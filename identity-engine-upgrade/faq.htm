Identity Engine upgrade FAQ

What is Okta ?

Okta is a new way to customize your Okta experience and implement flexible identity use cases. This means shifting from predefined behaviors to a platform that offers customizable building blocks for dynamic, app-based configurations.

Is my org eligible for the upgrade?

There are certain configuration tasks that you must complete before your org is eligible for self-service upgrade. Once the configurations are complete, the self-service upgrade notification appears on your Admin Dashboard and you can schedule your upgrade. See Eligibility tasks.

What is the general upgrade experience?

The self-service upgrade process ensures that your org's upgrade to is successful. Most upgrades only take a few minutes to complete, so there's no downtime for admins and end users. Your org configurations are seamlessly migrated to .

I don't have a Preview environment. How do I test before I upgrade?

Most upgrades have little or no impact on day-to-day operations. If you need to review the user experience changes, you can purchase a Preview environment or create an Identity Engine free trial, and view the new policy framework there.

I have a Preview environment. How do I test before I upgrade?

Upgrading in your Preview environment lets you assess before you upgrade your Production org. Be sure to align the configuration of your environments first, and then perform the upgrade in Preview. See Upgrade validation tests.
