Upgrade from

gives you increased control over how you protect your apps and resources and helps you solve complex identity use cases. There are several tasks that you need to complete before and after the upgrade to ensure your success with . Use this page to learn about the required upgrade tasks, find answers to frequently asked questions, and to understand the functionality changes for admins and users.

There are two ways you can upgrade your org to :

  • Arrange an upgrade through Okta Support. To find out if your org is eligible for an upgrade, check with your Okta representative. If you're eligible, they can arrange for Okta Support to upgrade your production org from Classic Engine to Identity Engine.

  • Schedule a self-service upgrade. If your org is eligible for self-service upgrade, you'll see a persistent notification in the Admin Dashboard to help you get started.
