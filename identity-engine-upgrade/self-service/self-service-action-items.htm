Self-service upgrade action items

Action items are acknowledgments or configurations that you must complete before you can schedule your upgrade to . To see a list of action items for your org, go to OIE Upgrade Hub in the Admin Console or click Upgrade to OIE on your Admin Dashboard.

If you don't see these self-service upgrade options in your dashboard, there may be some tasks that you need to complete before your org is eligible to upgrade. See Complete your eligibility tasks.
