Before you begin

Determine if you need to manually upgrade the Sign-In Widget:

  • If your org embeds the Sign-In Widget and you don't have other customizations, no action is required. Your Sign-In Widget is automatically upgraded to the latest version when it's loaded from the content delivery network (CDN).

  • If your org configured a custom domain and has other customizations, you must manually update the Sign-In Widget before you upgrade to . Continue with the task.
