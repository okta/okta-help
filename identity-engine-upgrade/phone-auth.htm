Phone authenticator

Learn how the phone authenticator changes after the upgrade.

Change summary The phone authenticator requires an external telephony service provider.
Admin experience

If your org had the phone authenticator enabled in , it’s migrated to . No action is needed.

If your org didn’t have the phone authenticator enabled in and you want to enable it in , you need an external telephony provider. You can connect it with using a telephony inline hook.

User experience If the phone authenticator isn’t configured in the org, it doesn't show up as an option during authenticator enrollment.
