Task 1: Identify the types of devices in your organization

During the migration, only desktop (Windows or macOS) devices are transferred to . There's no automated migration for mobile devices. Mobile devices use .

  1. Determine what types of devices you have. If Enable iOS or Enable Android is selected, you have mobile devices.

  2. Complete one of the following tasks, depending on the type of devices that you have:
    • Mobile (Android or iOS) and desktop (Windows or macOS) devices: go to Task 2.
    • Mobile devices only: go to Task 2.
    • Desktop devices only: go to Task 3.

Task 2: On , prepare mobile devices for migration

Mobile isn't eligible for migration to . Turn it off before you upgrade. After the upgrade, use or to ensure that mobile devices are trusted.

  1. Change the app sign-on policy condition for mobile devices to Any:

    2. Select the policy that you want to update.

Task 3: Upgrade from to

After the upgrade, your existing authentications (mutual TLS authentications) continue to work on .

When you upgrade, any app sign-on policy conditions are translated to Device Registered & Managed Conditions in . If the valid certificate is present on the device, the device is registered and managed. If a certificate doesn't exist on the device, or if it exists but isn't valid, the device is unmanaged.

To upgrade to , contact your account team. See Contact your Okta account team, and Give access to your Okta account team.
