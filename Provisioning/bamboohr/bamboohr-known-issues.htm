BambooHR integration known issues

These are the known issues when integrating BambooHR with :

  • New users can't be created in BambooHR. An error is returned when an user is assigned to BambooHR and provisioning is enabled. To avoid the error, import existing users from BambooHR first.

  • Unicode values replace special characters in attributes. For example, ShirtSize# is displayed as ShirtSize_U+0023.

  • Users who aren't employees can't be imported. However, you can provision the credentials of users who aren't employees.

  • Users who aren't employees can't be managed in .

  • The account used for provisioning must be a BambooHR administrator.
