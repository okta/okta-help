Create a Salesforce Portal integration

If you're using Salesforce portals, you can create an integration to provide access to a Portal subset of the Salesforce instance, where Portal users are provisioned as external users.

When a new Portal user is provisioned, creates a new contact in Salesforce associated with the Salesforce account. This new contact contains the user's name and email address. This contact is necessary because Portal users in Salesforce must be associated with a contact.
