Provision cloud applications

To manage provisioning actions between  and cloud applications, you can select SCIM-enabled app integrations in the  () or you can configure your own custom app integration. Two options are available for app provisioning:

  • Add a new app integration that has provisioning capabilities to your org. See Typical workflow for deploying new provisioning app integrations.
  • Configure provisioning on an existing Single Sign-On (SSO) app integration, or create a parallel provisioning-enabled app integration in your org. See Typical workflow for adding provisioning to an existing app integration.

After adding and configuring an app integration, you can assign the app to individual users, or to groups within your org.
