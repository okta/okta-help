Configure the

The interacts with the Devices SDK to allow you to configure your own branded push service and embed push notifications and biometric verification directly into your organization's mobile app. Your users don't have to leave your app to perform verification in another app when they attempt to sign in to your service.

Before you begin

Deploying custom push verification functionality is a multi-step process. You must perform several tasks in the Devices SDK before you can configure the notification services and the . See Custom Authenticator integration guide - Android or Custom Authenticator integration guide - iOS for prerequisites and instructions.
