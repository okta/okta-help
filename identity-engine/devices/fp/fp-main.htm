Overview

Devices register with Universal Directory through . After the one-time registration (regardless of where the user is located), the user has passwordless access to all resources in . This includes the , native mobile apps, SP-initiated browser access, and desktop thin clients that support modern authentication. It works on any device: managed or not managed, AD-Joined, or not AD-Joined. It doesn't require devices to be on an office network. Older desktop single sign-on (SSO) features in require (AD), but has no requirements for AD, other user-directories, or specific end-point management tools.

Once a device is registered, the user isn't prompted for a username or password when they try to log into their apps. The passwordless experience is controlled by the and configured by the admin.
