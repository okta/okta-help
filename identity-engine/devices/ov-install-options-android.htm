Deploy to Android devices

You can install on Android devices using one of the following methods:

  • For devices that are not managed, use one of the following methods:
    • Allow end users to download directly from Google Play.
    • Allow end users to download from a server location that you set up. This method is useful for users at locations where Google Play is not available to download from (for example, in China). In the Admin Console, go to Settings Downloads , click Download Latest for for Android, and then save the APK file on a server location that is accessible to users. Inform users that they must configure their devices to allow apps from sources other than Google Play before they can install .

    When Google services are not available, several features of are not functional. This includes, but is not limited to, the ability for end users to authenticate into your org using push notifications from .

    Okta strongly discourages off-market distribution of . Okta recommends that you install from Google Play whenever possible. Use an alternative installation method only when the Google Play is unavailable.

  • For devices that are managed, use your device management solution to deploy to devices, using one of the following methods:
    • Deploy [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] to devices from Google Play
    • Download [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] from Okta, and then deploy it to devices

Deploy to Android devices

Configure your device management tool to download from Google Play, and then deploy it to targeted end-user devices. This deployment method supports your device management tool's auto-update feature.

Download from Okta, and then deploy it to devices

  1. In the , go to Settings Downloads .
  2. For for Android, click Download Latest.

    An APK file with the latest version of for Android is downloaded.

  3. Configure your device management tool to deploy the app (APK file) to targeted end-user devices.

    This deployment method does not support auto-update. To deploy new versions of the app, manually download from the Okta , and then configure your device management tool to deploy the app to devices.
