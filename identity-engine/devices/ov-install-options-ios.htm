Deploy to iOS devices

You can deploy to iOS devices using one of the following methods:

  • Allow end users to download directly from Apple App Store.

  • Use your device management solution to deploy to devices from the Apple App Store.

    • Configure your device management tool to grab the app from the Apple App Store and deploy it directly to targeted end-user devices. This deployment method supports your device management tool's auto-update feature.
