Clone an authentication policy

Clone an authentication policy if you don't want to create a policy from scratch. A cloned policy duplicates the rules of the original policy but has no apps applied to it.

  2. Click Actions next to the policy that you want to clone.
  3. Select Clone policy.

The cloned policy appears with the prefix [cloned]. See Update an authentication policy to change its name, edit rules, and add apps.
