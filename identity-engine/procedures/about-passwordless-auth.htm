Configure passwordless authentication

You can configure the following types of a passwordless sign-in experiences for your end users:

Passwordless authentication with

Configure Device Trust on to distribute to managed devices and then configure your app sign-on and to support passwordless authentication. See Configure [%=okta-feature-names.Okta FastPass%] .

Passwordless authentication with email magic link

Enable email as an authenticator, configure your MFA enrollment policy accordingly, and then configure your app sign-on and to support passwordless authentication. See Configure [%=okta-feature-names.Okta FastPass%] .
