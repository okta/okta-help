password policies

enforces certain password requirements when changing the password of any user.

  • Minimum length of the password must be 8 characters.
  • Password must include:
    • At least 1 uppercase character.
    • At least 2 lowercase characters.
    • At least 2 numeric characters.
    • At least 1 special character.
  • The previous five passwords cannot be reused.
  • The account is locked after three failed sign-in attempts.
  • Locked accounts are unlocked after a period of 2 hours from the last failed sign in attempt.
