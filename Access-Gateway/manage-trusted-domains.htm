Manage trusted domains

Trusted domains are used to limit or specify a set of domains that is allowed to redirect to.

Using trusted domains, mistakes or attempts to hijack redirects are prohibited by the use of a list of known trusted sites.

Trusted domains are enabled by default in deployments of v2020.8.3 and later.

Trusted domains are disabled by default when upgrading from earlier versions to maintain existing system behavior.
