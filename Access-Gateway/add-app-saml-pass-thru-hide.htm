Hide applications

Hide the SAML app and from users by:

  2. Select the Direct access to . . . Okta app.
  3. Select the General tab.
  4. Click Edit.
  5. In the App Settings section, scroll to Application visibility and select Do not display application icon to users.
  6. Click Save.

To hide the app that you added during Create an [%=okta-feature-names.Access Gateway%] application, repeat the preceding steps, but select the app in step 2 instead of the SAML app.
