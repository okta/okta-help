Okta Groups for

All applications require one or more groups. These groups are used to grant or deny access to the protected applications resources.

You must define one or more groups that represent the sections of your application being protected in your Okta tenant. Complex policies may require you to create multiple groups.

Before you begin

  • You have administrator rights on your Okta tenant and can assign applications to users, and create groups.
