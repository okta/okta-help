Test the Oracle E-Business Suite integration

To test the Oracle E-Busimess Suite application integration.

  1. Select IDP Initiated from the drop-down box associated with the application.

    Your Okta tenant administrator may need to assign the application to you.

    In addition, if you do not already have an active Okta session open in your browser, you may be prompted to sign in to your Okta account and provide credentials.

  2. Verify that you are signed in to Oracle EBS correctly.
