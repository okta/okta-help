Okta groups for

All applications require one or more groups. These groups are used to grant or deny access to the protected applications resources. You must define one or more groups representing the sections of your application being protected in your Okta tenant. When a complex policy is required, multiple groups may be required.

Before you begin

Ensure that:

  • You have administrator rights on your Okta tenant.
  • You can assign applications to users and create groups.
