Configure this feature for your org

The Recently Used Apps feature is enabled by default for your org, which means that any user can turn it on in their Preferences menu. If you don't want to give users this option, disable the feature for your org.

Turn on this feature for your account

If the feature is enabled for your org, you can turn it on for your own account.

  1. On your dashboard, click the dropdown menu next to your name.

  2. Select Preferences.

  3. Click the Recently used toggle to turn it on.
