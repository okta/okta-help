Integrate Microsoft Teams with

This process requires a user account that has admin permissions for both and Teams Administrator permissions for a Microsoft organization.

Before you begin

  • Configure [%=okta-feature-names.Access Requests%]

Start this task

To integrate Microsoft Teams with , you must add the bot to your Microsoft Teams organization and then connect to a Microsoft account.

Add the bot to your Microsoft Teams organization

  1. Sign in to admin.teams.microsoft.com with an account that has Teams Administrator permissions.
  2. On the side menu, click Teams Apps Manage Apps .
  3. Locate the app.
  4. Select the app and click Add to team.

Connect to a Microsoft account

  1. Sign in to the Console with an account that has admin permissions.
  2. Go to Settings Integrations .
  3. Under Microsoft Teams, click Connect.
  4. In the Connect to Microsoft Teams modal, click Enable Microsoft Teams.
  5. Sign in to your Microsoft organization through the modal.

connects the app to the Microsoft organization.

Requesters can tag @okta in a chat that has the bot to submit requests. Alternatively, your users can locate the app in their Apps section and submit a request there. See Submit requests from [%=workflows_connectors.Microsoft Teams%].

After users interact with the bot or the app once, they automatically receive a notification when action is assigned to them or they're mentioned on a request.
