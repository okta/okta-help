Handle entitlement bundle requests

After the user submits a request, your approvers receive a notification. They must do the following to approve or deny the request.

Prerequisites for approvers

  • Approvers must sign in to and ensure that they're assigned to the Request Access application.

Steps for approvers

  1. From the Access Requests console, go to RequestsInbox.

  2. Select a request.

  3. Review the request from the Info and Questions tab.

  4. Go to the Tasks tab.

  5. Approve or deny the request.

After the approver approves the request, you can verify that the entitlements in the bundle were granted. To verify, perform the steps listed in Manage a user's entitlements or check the Events table in the System Log.

You can view the requests from the Past Access Requests report. Currently, you can’t filter the report for entitlement bundle requests.
