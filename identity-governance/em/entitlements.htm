Entitlements

An entitlement is a permission, privilege, or access level that allows users to take specific actions within a third-party app. After you enable , you can manage your user’s entitlements for a third-party app from , which allows you to monitor users’ access to resources, the level of access that they have within these resources, and how they were granted the access.

For provisioning-enabled apps, discovers entitlements automatically. Use policy rules to govern user entitlements.

You can assign entitlements to users by creating policy rules or assign them manually from the Admin Console. Group entitlements for an app into entitlement bundles. After you create entitlement bundles and set up Request Types in , users can also request entitlement bundles.
