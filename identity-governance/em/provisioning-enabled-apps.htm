Provisioning-enabled apps

Apps are provisioning-enabled if they can exchange entitlements between the app and . These apps have updated connectors to automatically discover entitlements and push user profile changes to the downstream app when the instance is properly configured to update users.

The following apps are provisioning-enabled:

  • Box

  • Google Workspace

  • Microsoft Office 365

  • NetSuite

  • Salesforce

There are ways to use with apps that don't have updated connectors. See Use Entitlement Management with apps without out-of-the-box governance support.

App-specific requirements

The following apps have specific requirements that you must meet to use with them:

  • Google Workspace requirements

  • NetSuite requirements

  • Salesforce requirements
