View entitlement policy rules

View existing entitlement policy rules to understand the criteria that determine entitlements granted to your users.

Before you begin

Sign in as a super admin and ensure that you're assigned to the   application.

Start this task

  2. In the Workflows console, go to the Flow tab.

  3. Open the Policy creation folder and go to the Flows subtab.

  4. Click View policy.

  5. Click Test.

  6. In the AppName field, enter the name of the application whose policy rules you want to view.

  7. Click Run Test.
