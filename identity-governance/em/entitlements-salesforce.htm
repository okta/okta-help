Salesforce requirements

  • requires that that you configure Salesforce to use OAuth for provisioning. See Configure OAuth and REST integration.

  • Only users with the profile entitlement can be assigned to Salesforce. A best practice to avoid assignment failures is to include the profile entitlement in commonly assigned bundles. This ensures that every user assigned to Salesforce has the required entitlement.
