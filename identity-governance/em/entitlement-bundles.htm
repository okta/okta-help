Entitlement bundles

Entitlement bundles allow you to grant multiple entitlements simultaneously to your users. Use Access Requests to allow your users to request entitlement bundles in a self-service manner.

You can’t assign entitlement bundles directly to your users from the Admin Console. Alternatively, you can use APIs to assign bundles to users. See Okta Identity Governance API.

Users must request entitlement bundles using Access Requests.
