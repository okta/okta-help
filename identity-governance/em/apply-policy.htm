Apply a policy

Applying a draft policy makes it active and assigns entitlements to users who meet its criteria.

Before you begin

  • Create one or more policy rules for the app.

  • As a best practice, preview the policy before you apply it.

Start this task

  1. Click Apply policy. Or, if you already have an active policy, click Continue editing policy, and then click Apply policy.

This process usually takes some time to complete. Users’ entitlements aren’t updated immediately.

Entitlements are assigned to users based on the policy rules that you set up.

You can view a user's assigned entitlements from their profile page or from the app’s assignments. See Manage user's entitlements.
