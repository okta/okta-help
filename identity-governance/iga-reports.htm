Identity Governance Reports

Use these reports to:

  • Obtain a high-level overview of past certification campaigns, including their duration and resources included.

  • See details of past resource access requests and campaigns, including users involved and remediation status.

  • Help meet audit and compliance requirements.

For more information see Access Certifications.

Several other Okta reports may also be helpful in meeting audit and compliance requirements. See Entitlements and Access Reports.
