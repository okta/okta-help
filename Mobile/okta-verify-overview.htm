is a multifactor authentication (MFA) app developed by . It lets users verify their identity when they sign in to and makes it less likely that someone pretending to be the user can gain access to the account.

To use , you must first enable and configure it for your org, and then your end users must install the app on their device and set it up. Then, when end users sign in to , they can verify their identity by approving a push notification in the app, or by entering a one-time code provided by the app into .
