Admin Configuration

To enable the Okta Mobile Safari Extension,

  1. From the AdminAn abbreviation of administrator. This is the individual(s) who have access to the Okta Administrator Dashboard. They control the provisioning and deprovisioning of end users, the assigning of apps, the resetting of passwords, and the overall end user experience. Only administrators have the Administration button (shown below) on the upper right side of the My Applications page. Dashboard, click to the Security drop-down menu.
  2. On the drop-down menu, choose the General page.
  3. On the General page, scroll down to the Okta Mobile section.
  4. Under Okta Extension for iOS , note the Sign on to SAML apps check-box.

The Sign on to SAML apps check box allows the Safari Extension to share the Okta Mobile session with Safari. Essentially, an end user can sign into SAML apps without re-entering their Okta credentials on their mobile device.

This option is turned on by default, but can be disabled if you’d rather not allow seamless SAML access to Safari. If you choose to disable, please note that only SWA apps will activate. For end users, a message in their Safari mobile browser indicates that SAML apps are not supported.

End-User Configuration

To enable the Okta Mobile Safari Extension, end users must perform a one-time configuration.

  1. Tap the open-in button at the bottom of the Mobile Safari browser.

  1. Swipe right to reach the end of the options, then tap the More button.

  1. Locate the Okta Mobile Safari Extension, enable it, then drag it to the top of the list.

  1. The extension now appears when you tap the open-in button in Mobile Safari.
