User Accounts report

Use this report to view users with accounts in Okta and their profile information. You can also use it to determine which users can authenticate with Okta.

  • You have users in your org who have accounts in Okta.
  • Your browser’s pop-up blocker is disabled.

Get report

  1. In the , go to ReportsReports.
  2. In the Entitlements and Access section, click User Accounts.
  1. Click Edit Filters.
  1. Select one of the following fields, choose an operator, and then enter an appropriate value:
