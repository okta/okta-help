Administrators

Okta administrators (or admins) are responsible for maintaining all aspects of the end-user experience in their orgs. There are many types of admins, and each has a unique set of permissions and restrictions. What most Okta admins have in common is their access to the , where they perform administrative tasks like user lifecycle management, application provisioning, and org customization.
