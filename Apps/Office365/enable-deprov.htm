Enable deprovisioning in Office 365

This topic explains how to deprovision users from the Office 365 app.

Before you begin

  • Review Deprovisioning options for Office 365.

Start this task

  1. Go to Office 365 Provisioning .
  2. Enable Deactivate Users.

    The Microsoft Office 365 user status on deactivation drop-down menu appears.

  3. From the drop-down menu, choose an appropriate option. See Deprovisioning options for Office 365.

    For Block sign-in and remove licenses after grace period only, License removal grace period (days) appears. Enter the grace period.

  4. Click Save.
