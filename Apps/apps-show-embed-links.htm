Copy the embed link for an app integration

You can use an app integration's embed link to add an Okta-managed application to a portal or other external location.

  2. Select the app integration that you want to embed.
  3. Click the General tab.
  4. Select and copy the Embed Link URL. Paste it into your portal or other external location outside of Okta.

Your users can now use this link to sign in to this app from outside of Okta.
