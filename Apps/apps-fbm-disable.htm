Disable Federation Broker Mode

Disable Federation Broker Mode if one of the Federation Broker Mode known limitations becomes a requirement for your application.

If you have a large number of group assignments, disabling Federation Broker Mode can consume substantial processing resources and take a significant time to complete.

  2. Enter the application name in the Search field.
  3. Select the application and click the General tab.
  4. Scroll down to Federation Broker Mode and click Edit.
  5. Click Enable Federation Broker Mode.
  6. Review the information in the dialog box that appears and click Continue.

  7. Click Save.

Federation Broker Mode is not disabled immediately. Until the background process is complete, the app remains in Federation Broker Mode to ensure that your end users do not lose app access. When the process is completed, hidden tabs and user assignments are restored.
