Okta End-User Dashboard

The Okta End-User Dashboard offers end users immediate access to apps.

When you provision an app, it's automatically added to the user dashboard. Various customization options are available for end users. For example, they can place recently used apps at the top of the page for easy access. The apps that are listed on the dashboard are also available directly from the .
