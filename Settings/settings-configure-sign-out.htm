Customize a sign-out page

By default, users who sign out of Okta are returned to your sign-in page. Use a custom sign-out page if you want to redirect them to a specific URL.

  2. Select a brand.
  3. On the Settings tab, scroll to the Sign-Out Page section, and then click Edit.
  4. Select Use a custom sign out page and enter the sign-out page URL.
  5. Click Save.
