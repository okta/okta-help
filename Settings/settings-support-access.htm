Give access to Okta Support

Giving administrator access to Okta Support allows them to sign in to your org and troubleshoot issues. Super admins can grant this access for 8 hours or for additional 24-hour increments.

  2. In the Give Access to Okta Support section, click Edit.
  3. Set the option Okta Support access to Enabled.
  4. Click Save. Okta Support now has 8 hours of administrator access to your org.
  5. To extend Okta Support's access by 24 hours, click Edit, and then click Extend access by 1 day.
  6. To end Okta Support's access before the expiration, click Edit, and then set the option to Disabled.
