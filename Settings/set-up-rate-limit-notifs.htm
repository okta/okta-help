Set up rate limit notifications

All admins are notified by the Rate Limit Monitoring widget on the Admin Dashboard and by email about their org-wide rate limit usage. See Rate limits overview.

Admins aren't notified of client and operations-based rate limit usage.

Notifications include:

  • Warnings when the org's rate limit usage exceeds the threshold. Currently, the threshold is set at 60% for API products and at 90% for workforce products.

  • Violations when the org's rate limit usage reaches 100%.

You can access the Rate Limits Dashboard from these notifications to understand and investigate the rate limit and the current utilization of each endpoint. See Rate Limits Dashboard.

Before you begin

Ensure that you're signed in as an admin.

Enable rate limit notifications

  1. In the , go to Settings Account .
  2. In the Rate Limiting Communications section, click Edit.
  3. Select the Enable Rate Limiting Communications check box to enable emails about your org-wide rate limit usage.
  4. Click Save.

Unsubscribe from email notifications

  1. In the , go to Settings Account .
  2. In the Admin Email Notifications section, click Edit.
  3. Clear the Rate limit warning and violation check box.
  4. Click Save.
