Enable the Directories Debugger

The Directories Debugger collects diagnostic data from AD and LDAP agents. If you enable the Directories Debugger and give access, they can use the data to troubleshoot agent issues. You can then disable access when it's no longer required.
Access is granted for 8 hours by default, but you can extend it in 24-hour increments to a maximum of 14 days.
