Deploy your connector

The Deployment tab enables you to:

  • Validate your connector.

  • Make a new test version of a connector.

  • Promote your test version into a private deployment. This connector can only be used in production flows on your associated org.

  • Submit a test version for review. After the team reviews, approves, and publishes your connector on the , any customer can deploy your connector within their org.

This tab is organized into three sections to help you complete your deployment tasks:

  • Test connector versions

  • Private deployments

  • Deploy your connector
