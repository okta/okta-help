Submit your connector

If your tested and working connector is ready for inclusion in the , you can submit your connector directly through .

To submit your connector to the :

  1. Click Submit connector.

  2. The Submit connector to Okta dialog outlines the requirements necessary to complete a connector submission. You need:

    • A link to the user documentation for your connector.

    • A valid email address for customer support communication.

    • An Okta org with sufficient data available to properly evaluate the connector function.

    • A file containing the exported test flows.

    • A document that details the tests covered by the exported test flows.

    • A link to the documentation for the API used to build your connector.

    See submissions for details regarding these requirements.

  3. Click Continue to .

  4. [The following steps were removed by OKTA-603840 because Connector Builder is now hooked into the console - they can be removed - confirm with Flora Chen]

  5. The Submit connector to Okta dialog outlines the details of your submission to .

    The submission process pulls these details from the Overview tab of your connector. If any changes are needed, click Cancel, update the fields in the Overview tab, and then resubmit your connector.

    You can choose which Connector version to submit from the dropdown menu. Confirm the Contact information for the team can contact you directly about your submission.

  6. Click Submit and Done to complete the submission process.

Submission details

The panel shows a summary of your submission to the :

  • Created date: The date and time when you sent in the submission for your connector through .

  • Version submitted: Which test version was submitted to the .

  • Last modified by: Who submitted the connector or made the most recent update to an existing submission.

  • Status: A submission moves through the following stages:

    • Editing

    • Cancelled

    • Initial Review Started

    • Initial Review Complete

    • Okta QA Started

    • Okta QA Complete

    • Okta Final QA Review

    • Customer Test

    • Sign Off

    • Ready to Publish

    • Published

    • Okta Discarded

Click View in OIN Manager to launch a browser tab showing the with the current details of your submission.

After a connector reaches the publication stage, you can use the to submit newer versions of your connector. The existing connector remains in place in the catalog until publishes the newer version.
