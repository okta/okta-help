Authorization

When you add a <new-connector> card to a flow for the first time, you'll be prompted to configure the connection. This will enable you to connect your <new-connector> account, save your account information, and reuse the connection for future <new-connector> flows.

You can create multiple connections and manage them from your Connections page.

To create a new connection from an Action or Event card:

  1. Click New Connection.

  2. Enter an Connection Nickname. Make this nickname unique so you can authorize multiple accounts.

  3. Click Create.

  4. Additional steps.

Supported Scopes

The following OAuth scopes must be enabled in your <connector-name> environment:

  • scope1

  • scope2

  • scope3
