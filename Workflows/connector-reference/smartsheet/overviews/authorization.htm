Authorization

To get started working with the application, the first step is to integrate an existing account with Workflows. If you do not have an existing account and you wish to use with Workflows, sign up for first, and then return to the authorization process.

Tip

You can create multiple connections and manage them from your Connections page.

Create a new Smartsheet connection in Workflows

Create a new connection from an Event or Action card.

For information about permissions, see Guidance for Adobe User Management connector

  1. Click New Connection.

  2. Enter a Connection Nickname. This is useful if you plan to create multiple connections to share with your team.

  3. Click Create.

  4. In the authorization dialog, enter the email address that is associated with your account.

  5. Click Allow to authorize access to your account.

If you're having difficulty connecting to your account, try reauthorizing your account. To do this, go to Connections, or delete your account and add it again.
