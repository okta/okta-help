Update Company

Update an existing company using a company ID. This card will update the fields with the values entered in the input fields.

Input

The fields that populate under the 'Company' header are dynamically generated, based on the company form properties defined in your Hubspot account.

Field Definition Type Required
Update By FALSE
Company ID  The ID of the company that is being updated. Number FALSE
Fields FALSE
Dynamically Generated Inputs determined by company form properties. These are defined by the user(s) of the account. String FALSE

NOTE: Because of changes to the labels of certain fields in the Hubspot API, it may be necessary to regenerate inputs and/or outputs on cards by clicking the gear icon at the bottom right corner of the card and selecting "Choose fields." This change affected the "Hubspot Owner" field, which may have been changed to "Company Owner," "Deal Owner," or "Contact Owner" depending on the card. If you created your card after May 9th, your card will have generated the currently correct inputs and/or outputs.
