Add Contact to Company

Add an existing contact to an existing company.

Input Fields

Field Definition Type Required
Company
Company ID  The unique identifier of the company you're adding the contact to. (this ID can be found by going to the company in HubSpot and looking for the numbers at the end of the URL. For example, .../company/459044660/) Number FALSE
Contact
Contact ID The unique identifier of the contact you're adding to the company specified. (this ID can be found by going to the contact in HubSpot and looking for the numbers at the end of the URL. For example, .../contact/101/) Number FALSE
