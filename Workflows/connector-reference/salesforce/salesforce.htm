errors

See Status Codes and Error Responses for more information on specific errors returned from the API.

If you receive a generic error like "Bad Request" from , look for the actual error message in the object that is returned by the API.

Authentication errors

If you have trouble connecting to your account, see your available Salesforce accounts and reauthorize your account on the Connections page, or delete your account and add it again.
