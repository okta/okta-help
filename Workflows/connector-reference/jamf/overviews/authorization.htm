When you add a card to a for the first time, you'll be prompted to configure the connection. This will enable you to connect your account, save your account information, and reuse the connection in new that include .

Prerequisites

Make sure that your account has the following settings:

  • Type: Standard User

  • Access Level: Full Access

  • Privileges: Administrator

Make sure that your account has access to the computercommands endpoint.

Make sure that your account has access to the /JSSResource/activationcode endpoint.

Tip

You can create multiple connections and manage them from your Connections page.

Create a Jamf connection

To set up a new connection in :

  1. Click New Connection.
  2. Enter a Connection Nickname. This should be unique. This is a helpful feature to enable you to differentiate between multiple connections.
  3. In the Username field, enter your username.
  4. In the Password field, enter your password.
  5. In the Full Instance URL field, enter the url. For example, https://instance.jamfcloud.com.
  6. Click Create to launch a pop-up window where you will sign into your Account. This will also save your connection.
