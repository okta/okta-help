Authorization

Authorize this connector by creating a connection to your account. When you add an card to a flow for the first time, you'll be prompted to create a connection. You can reuse this connection the next time that you build a flow with this connector.

You can create multiple connections and manage them from your Connections page.

Prerequisites

  • Okta Workflows administrator credentials

  • admin credentials or a system role credentials with permissions to access the company API Credential Manager.

  • A SmartRecruit account that contains the SmartConnect add-on which enables account holders to access to the Developer APIs.

To configure connector in Workflows:

  1. Do one of the following:

    • In the Okta Workflows Console, go to Connections New Connection .
    • From a event or action card, click New Connection.

  2. Enter a Connection Nickname. This is useful if you plan to create multiple connections to share with your team.

  3. Click Create.

  4. In the dialog box:

  • Enter your email address and click Next.

  • Enter your password and click Log in.
