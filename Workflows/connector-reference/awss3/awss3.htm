AWS S3 connector

Amazon Simple Storage Service (S3) is an object storage service that offers industry-leading scalability, data availability, security, and performance. The connector allows you to retrieve and modify your resources.

  • Authorize your account
  • connector action cards
  • Supported scopes

Requirements

AWS account with S3 and IAM services enabled.

Revoke Access

To revoke access the user must delete the associated trust policy or IAM role created at connection creation time.

Authorize your account

When you add an card to a for the first time, you'll be prompted to configure the connection. This enables you to connect your AWS account, save your account information, and reuse the connection for future flows. See Authorization.
