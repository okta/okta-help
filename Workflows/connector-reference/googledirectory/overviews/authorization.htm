Authorization

When you add a card to a flow for the first time, prompts you to configure the connection. This connection links to your account and saves your account information so you can reuse the connection for future flows.

To create a new connection from an action card, complete the following steps:

  1. Click New Connection.

  2. Enter a Connection Nickname. As a best practice, make this nickname unique so that you can connect multiple accounts.

  3. Enter your Domain.

  4. Click Create.
