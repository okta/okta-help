connector

provides tools to manage access and authentication based on identity. You can create, update, list, and delete users, add users to groups, assign users to apps, and make custom calls to the API.

Authorize your account

When you add an card to your flow, you need to authorize an account from your org. See Authorization.

Revoke tokens

When you've deleted a user or a connection to your org, the connector revokes all associated access tokens. You don't have to delete the tokens manually. See Revoke Token.

Guidance for connector

Refer to this information for guidance and best practices when using the connector in your flows. See Guidance for Okta connector.

For information about how Okta connector cards impact the rate limits for your environment, see Workflows system limits.
