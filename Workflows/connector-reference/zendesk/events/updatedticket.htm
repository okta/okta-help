Updated Ticket

This card monitors for tickets that are updated within .

It takes up to 15 minutes for new or updated records in to hit the API, meaning the designer can't read new or updated records for at least 15 minutes after the time they are created. This means that the Run Now feature won't work for testing flows that start with a monitor. Please wait 20 minutes after creating or updating records for the monitor to run naturally in order to test monitors in your flows.

Output

Any custom fields that exist will appear under 'Record'.
