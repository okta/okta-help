When you add a card to a flow for the first time, you'll be prompted to configure the connection. This will enable you to connect your account, save your account information, and reuse the connection in new flows that include .

Tip

You can create multiple connections and manage them from your Connections page.

Create a new connection from an Action card

  1. Click New Connection.

  2. Enter a Connection Nickname. This is useful if you plan to create multiple connections to share with your team.

  3. Enter a Subdomain for your instance. The value for the Zendesk subdomain cannot contain a - dash character. For example, if the URL for your instance is mywork.zendesk.com, then the value for the Zendesk subdomain is mywork only.

  4. Click Create. The Sign In dialog appears.

  5. In the Sign In dialog, enter an email address and password, then click Sign In, to log in to your account.

  6. If prompted, click Allow.
