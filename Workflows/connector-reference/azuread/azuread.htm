connector

APIs allow you to manage your licenses, users, and groups. You can create, read, update, and delete users and groups, and use a combination of APIs to manage licenses.

Authorize your account

The connector uses OAuth2 for authentication and authorization. The first time you add an card to a flow, you're prompted to create a connection. See Authorization.
