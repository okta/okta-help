Delegation

Users must be an admin, have delegated access, or be part of a group to access files not owned by the authenticated user.

Authorize your account

The connector uses OAuth 2.0 for authentication and authorization. The first time you add a card to a flow, you're prompted to create a connection. See Authorization.
