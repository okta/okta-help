Authorization

When you add a card to a flow for the first time, you'll be prompted to configure the connection. This will enable you to connect your Box account, save your account information, and reuse the connection for future Box flows.

You can create multiple connections and manage them from your Connections page.

To create a new connection from an Action card:

  1. Click New Connection.

  2. Enter a Connection Nickname. This is useful if you plan to create multiple connections to share with your team.

  3. Click Create.

  4. Log in to your account to authorize the connection.

  5. In , click Grant Access. This action saves your connection and returns you to your flow.

Supported Scopes

The following OAuth scopes must be enabled in your environment:

  • Read all files and folders stored in

  • Read and write all files and folders stored in

  • Manage users

  • Manage groups

  • Manage webhooks

  • Manage enterprise properties

  • Manage retention policies

  • Enable integrations
