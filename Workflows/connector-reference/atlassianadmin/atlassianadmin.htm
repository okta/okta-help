Atlassian admin accounts allow you to perform lifecycle management tasks in Atlassian products, including creating, updating, and deactivating users and groups.

Authorize your account

When you add an Atlassian card to a flow for the first time, you'll be prompted to configure the connection. This will enable you to connect your Atlassian account, save your account information, and reuse the connection in new flows that include Atlassian. See Authorization.
