Authorization

To use any card in a flow, you must configure a connection. This will enable you to connect your account, save your account information, and reuse the connection in other flows that contain cards.

To create a new connection from an Action card:

  1. Click New Connection.

  2. Enter a Connection Nickname. This is useful if you plan to create multiple connections to share with your team.

  3. Add your domain in the Domain field. URLs are in the format https://<auth.domain>.myshopify.com, where your domain is <auth.domain>. Enter this value in the Domain field.

  4. Click Create. A authentication window is launched.

  5. Log in to your account to authorize the connection.

  6. In , click Grant Access. This saves your connection and returns you to your flow.
