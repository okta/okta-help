Authorization

When you add an card to a flow for the first time, you'll be prompted to configure the connection. You'll be able to connect your Asana account, save your account information, and reuse the connection in new flows that include . The connector uses OAuth protocol.

Tip

You can create multiple connections and manage them from your Connections page.

Create a new connection from an Event or Action card

  1. Click New Connection.

  2. Enter a Connection Nickname. This is useful if you plan to create multiple connections to share with your team.

  3. Optionally, add a token in the Service Account Token field. This is necessary if you want to retrieve information such as secondary email addresses when using the Read User or Search User cards.

  4. Click Create.

  5. In the Sign In dialog, enter an email address and password, then click Sign In, to log in to your account.

Optionally, you can log in with a Google account, or use your corporate single sign-on credentials.

  1. Click Allow to authorize access to your account.

Configure connector in Workflows

  1. In the Okta Workflows Console, go to Connections.

  2. Click New Connection. Available connectors pop up in a window.

  3. Select .

  4. Enter the Connection Nickname.

  5. Click Create. The OAuth Login window opens.

  6. Sign in to using admin credentials. You are prompted to authorize the Asana connector.

  7. Click Authorize.

The connector is now configured and this connection is ready to be used with available action cards for the connector.

Deauthorize or Uninstall connector in Workflows

You can delete individual connections to deauthorize the Asana application connector or uninstall the entire Asana application connector.

Delete a connection in Workflows

  1. In the Okta Workflows Console, go to Connections.

  2. Click Delete next to the connection you want to delete. The Delete Connection dialog appears.

  3. On the window, click Confirm.

The connection is now deleted.
