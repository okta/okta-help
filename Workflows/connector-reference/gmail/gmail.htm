Authorize your account

The connector uses OAuth 2.0 for authentication and authorization. The first time you add a Gmail card to a , you're prompted to create a connection. See Authorization.

You may experience intermittent connection outages when you create a new connection for the connector. Existing connections and flows that include Gmail connector event or action cards are not affected. See Gmail connection returning "This app is blocked" error message.

Transfer of Ownership

To use transfer of ownership features with Workflows, see Authorize an Account for Transfer of Ownership Features.
