Step 2: Build the helper flow

  1. In the For Each card, click Choose Flow.

  2. In the folder window, click New Flow.

  3. Enter the name Import Users - Helper Flow.

  4. Click Add New Event, and then select Helper Flow from the Okta Apps menu.

  5. In the Inputs to this Flow field, enter Rows. This will map the Rows field from your parent flow to your helper flow.

  6. Save the helper flow and all of its data. If you get an error, verify that the helper flow is configured correctly, and then try again.
