Step 4: Trigger the flow

  1. In the Admin Console, go to Directory Directory Integrations .

  2. Select Active Directory, and then click the Import tab.

  3. Click Import Now.

  4. In the Workflows console, click Flow History.

  5. Verify that each card in your flow has a success check mark.

  6. Verify that your imported users have unique email addresses.

It takes about 60 seconds for the flow to enable. After saving and turning on your flow, wait one minute before you attempt to trigger it.
