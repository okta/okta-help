Step 2: Trigger the flow

  1. In the Admin Console, go to Directory People .
  2. Select a user, and in their More Actions menu, click Suspend.
  3. In the Workflows console, click Flow History.
  4. Verify that each card in your flow has a success check mark.
  5. Verify that you received the User Suspended Notification email.

It takes about 60 seconds for the flow to enable. After saving and turning on your flow, wait one minute before you attempt to trigger it.
