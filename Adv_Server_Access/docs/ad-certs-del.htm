Delete a certificate

Teams can delete certificates from the dashboard. Teams can’t delete a certificate associated with Active Directory connections. Teams must first update the connections to use another certificate or authentication method. See Add a certificate to an AD connection.

  1. Open the dashboard.
  2. In the user menu, click Team Settings.
  3. Go to the Passwordless Certificates tab. 
  4. Identify the created certificate record.
  5. Click > Delete.
  6. In the confirmation window, click Delete.
