Remove a group from Active Directory provisioning

When you remove an Active Directory (AD) group, identifies the next priority AD group. If the group has a different Organizational Unit (OU) assigned to it than the group you removed, updates the users to the new OU.

  1. In the , go to Directory Groups .
  2. Click a group name.
  3. Click Manage Directories.
  4. In the Members list, click an AD directory.
  5. Click Next and then Confirm Changes.
