Test the Desktop Single Sign-on settings

  1. In the , go to Security Delegated Authentication Agentless DSSO .
  2. Scroll to Agentless Desktop SSO and confirm that On is selected for Desktop SSO mode.
  3. Sign in to an active Windows account that uses .
  4. Open an internet browser, enter https://<myorg>.okta.com/login/agentlessDssoMigrationReadiness in the address bar, and press Enter.

    If the DSSO settings are correct and the org is ready, the user home page appears. Contact support and indicate your org is ready to migrate and have the agentless DSSO functionality enabled. When you submit your request, in the Subject line paste TC: ADSSO Reg Key Deprecation. If you cannot access the URL, contact support and request assistance with your configuration.
