Manage OMM Devices

You can view users enrolled in Okta Mobility Management (OMM) in your org on the Okta Mobility Management page. It provides an overview of device enrollment status, security status, operating system (OS) type, and 30-day activity. You can also generate a device report and download it as a CSV file.
