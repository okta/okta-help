Create an Okta support case

  1. In the Okta , click the ? icon, and then click Help Center.

  2. Click Get Support, located in the lower-right corner, and then click Open a Case.

  3. Enter the following information:

    • Subject*

    • Detailed Description*

    • Steps to Reproduce Business Impact Priority*

    • Okta Org Type*

    • Okta Org*

    • Case Email*

    • Phone No.

    • Add Contact to Team

    • Add Attachment

    * This information is required.
