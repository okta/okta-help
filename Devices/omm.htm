Mobility Management

Okta Mobility Management (OMM) allows you to manage your end users' computers, mobile devices, applications, and data. Your end users enroll in the service, download managed apps from the Apps Store, and then use the apps. Managed apps are typically work-related; for example, Box, and Concur. As an administrator, you can remove managed apps and associated data from end users' devices at any time. You can also configure policies, such as data sharing controls, on any of your managed apps.
