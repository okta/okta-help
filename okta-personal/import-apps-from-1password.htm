Import apps from 1Password

When you import passwords from 1Password into Okta Personal, you instantly add those apps to your Okta Personal dashboard. This is a faster method than manually adding apps through the plugin or dashboard.

How it works

Importing apps from 1Password is a two-step process. First, export your 1Password passwords as a CSV file, and then import that file into Okta Personal.

Okta Personal imports app names, usernames, passwords, and URLs only. Other data that you store with 1Password isn't imported into Okta Personal.

Before you begin

Create an account

Set up MFA

Install the Okta Browser Plugin

Import apps

  1. Export your saved passwords from 1Password. See 1Password documentation.

  2. In a desktop browser, go to your Okta Personal dashboard.

  3. Open the menu next to your name, and then select Import apps.

  4. In the Browsers & password managers section, choose 1Password.

  5. Click Choose File.

  6. Select your 1Password CSV file.

  7. Click Import.

  8. After your apps are imported, click Go to My Apps to see them on your Okta Personal dashboard.

  9. Delete the 1Password CSV file.
