Sign in to apps with Okta Personal

Signing in to Okta Personal gives you access to all of your saved apps. You only have to enter your app credentials the first time you sign in.

Apps that you launch from your Okta Personal dashboard or the Okta Browser Plugin always open in a new browser window.

Sign in from the dashboard

  1. In a desktop browser, go to your Okta Personal dashboard.

  2. Click an icon to launch the app.

Sign in from the plugin

This method lets you sign in to apps from any Chrome browser window (as long as you’ve signed in to Okta Personal first).

  1. In a desktop browser, click the puzzle icon in your taskbar.

  2. Select Okta Browser Plugin from the list of extensions.

  3. Click an icon to launch the app.

If the plugin doesn't autofill your credentials, you can copy your username or password from the plugin.

  1. In a desktop browser, click the puzzle icon in your taskbar.

  2. Select Okta Browser Plugin from the list of extensions.

  3. Find the app you want to open and click its View details menu (the three dots).

  4. Click Copy next to your username or password.

  5. Paste the copied value into the app sign-in fields.

Sign in on iOS devices

This method requires AutoFill enabled for Okta Personal.

  1. Sign in to the Okta Personal app on your iPhone.

  2. In a Safari browser, enter the URL of the app you want to open.

  3. Go to the app's sign-in page, and then tap the username field.

  4. Tap the key icon.

  5. Select the app you're trying to access from your Okta Personal apps list.

  6. When you see your credentials on the app sign-in page, tap Sign in.

The next time you visit this app in your Safari browser, you're prompted to sign in using Okta Personal. Tap Use <your username> to sign in.
