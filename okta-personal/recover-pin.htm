Recover your PIN

Okta Personal doesn't currently support PIN recovery. You're the only one who knows your PIN.

If you're unable to sign in, you have two options:

  1. Try a different PIN that you use for other apps.
  2. Email okta_personal@okta.com to reset your PIN. Resetting your PIN deletes your current apps, so after you set up a new PIN, you need to add your apps again.
