Enter your PIN

There are only a few times that you’re prompted to enter your PIN after the initial setup. You're prompted when you sign in to your account on a new device or browser, when you refresh the page or open the dashboard in a new tab, and when you install the Okta Browser Plugin.

PIN setup is available through a desktop browser only. The passcode on your iPhone doesn’t replace your Okta Personal PIN.

Before you begin

Set up a PIN

Enter your PIN when you sign in

You're prompted for your PIN when you sign in to your account on a new device or browser, or if you refresh your browser.

  1. Sign in to Okta Personal on your iPhone or a desktop browser.

  2. Enter your six-digit PIN.

Enter your PIN when you install the plugin

You're prompted to create or enter your PIN when you install the Okta Browser Plugin.

  1. Sign in to Okta Personal on your iPhone or a desktop browser.

  2. Install the Okta Browser Plugin.

  3. Click Next.

  4. Enter your six-digit PIN.

Enter your PIN in the plugin

If you signed in to your account without entering a PIN, you're prompted for it when you try to use the Okta Browser Plugin.

  1. In a desktop browser, click the puzzle icon in your taskbar.

  2. Click Unlock account.

  3. Enter your six-digit PIN.

  4. Click Unlock.
