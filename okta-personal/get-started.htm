Get started with Okta Personal

Okta Personal is the Okta identity offering for personal use. Okta Personal allows you to see all of your apps in one place, securely store your passwords, and autofill your credentials when you sign in to apps.

How does it work?

Okta Personal is like the Okta dashboard that you use for work. However, unlike Okta Workforce, Okta Personal is owned and managed by you. You can store and manage your personal information and who has access to it. Data that you share with Okta Personal is shared with third-party apps only with your authorized consent.

Okta Personal allows you to:

  • See all of your apps in one place

  • Securely store, save, and autofill your passwords

  • Manage what data you share and revoke access anytime*

  • Sign in to your websites as a social login*

  • Sign up on new websites without creating an account*
