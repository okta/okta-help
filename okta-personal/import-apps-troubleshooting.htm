Troubleshoot app imports

If you have trouble moving apps from your workforce account, check the following issues.

I don't see any of these options on my phone.

You can't import apps on a mobile device. This feature is for desktop browsers only.

I don't see the plugin icon in my desktop browser taskbar.

Click Manage Extensions. Toggle the Okta Browser Plugin switch to enable it.

The Import apps option isn't in my menu.

Verify that your Okta Workforce account appears in the browser plugin and that you signed in to it with your workforce credentials. See Link your accounts.

I selected Import apps but I don't see any apps.

Verify that you have personal apps on your work dashboard. Apps that use a work domain aren't eligible to move.

If you have eligible apps but they don't appear on your Import apps page, be sure that your org allows moving personal apps.

Some of the apps I imported didn't move.

Check the number of apps that you tried to move. You can't move more than 100 apps from your Okta Workforce account.
