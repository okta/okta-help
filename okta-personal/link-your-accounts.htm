Link your accounts

Use the browser plugin to link your Okta Workforce and Okta Personal accounts. Linking your accounts enables the import feature and shows you which apps are eligible to move.

Before you begin

Install the Okta Browser Plugin

Add your Okta Workforce account to the plugin

  1. In a desktop browser, go to your Okta Personal dashboard.

  2. Click the plugin icon in your browser taskbar. If you don’t see it, click Manage Extensions and enable the plugin by toggling the switch.

  3. Click the arrow icon in the bottom-left corner.

  4. Click Add company of other account.

  5. Enter your company URL, and then complete the sign-in flow. Use your workforce credentials here.

  6. Open the plugin, and then click Add.
