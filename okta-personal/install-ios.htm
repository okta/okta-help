Install Okta Personal for iOS

Okta Personal is available on iOS mobile devices running version 15.0 or later, and it requires the Apple TestFlight app. TestFlight is a beta-testing app that provides usage metrics to Okta and lets you send feedback.

Before you can install these apps, create your account and set up MFA in a desktop browser. Then, use your public link to install both Apple TestFlight and Okta Personal Wallet on your iPhone. If you'd rather install TestFlight manually, you can also get it from the App Store.

Use your public link to install TestFlight

  1. Send your public link to a location that you can access on your iPhone.

  2. Tap the link.

  3. Tap View in App Store.

  4. Download the TestFlight app, and then tap Open.

  5. On the welcome screen, tap Continue.

  6. When you see Okta Personal Wallet in the list, tap Install.

Install TestFlight from the App Store

  1. Download the TestFlight app from the App Store, and then tap Open.

  2. On the welcome screen, tap Continue.

  3. Tap the Okta Personal link on your Apple device.

  4. Tap Accept.

  5. When you see Okta Personal Wallet in the list, tap Install.
