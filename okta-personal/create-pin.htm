Set up a PIN

PIN setup is available through a desktop browser only during the beta release.

After you create your account, set up a PIN to encrypt your apps. Combining your account password with a PIN protects your account so that no one else can access your data. Be sure to use a PIN that you won't forget.

Before you begin

Create an account

Create a PIN

  1. In a desktop browser, sign in to your Okta Personal account.
  2. On the PIN page, enter and confirm a six-digit PIN.
  3. Click Next.
