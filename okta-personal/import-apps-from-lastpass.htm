Import apps from LastPass

When you import passwords from LastPass into Okta Personal, you instantly add those apps to your Okta Personal dashboard. This is a faster method than manually adding them through the plugin or dashboard.

How it works

Importing apps from LastPass is a two-step process. First, export your LastPass passwords as a CSV file, and then import that file into Okta Personal.

Okta Personal imports app names, usernames, passwords, and URLs only. Other data that you store with LastPass isn't imported into Okta Personal.

Before you begin

Create an account

Set up MFA

Install the Okta Browser Plugin

Import apps

  1. Export your saved passwords from LastPass. See LastPass documentation.

  2. In a desktop browser, go to your Okta Personal dashboard.

  3. Open the menu next to your name, and then select Import apps.

  4. In the Browsers & password managers section, choose LastPass.

  5. Click Choose File.

  6. Select your LastPass CSV file.

  7. Click Import.

  8. After your apps are imported, click Go to My Apps to see them on your Okta Personal dashboard.

  9. Delete the LastPass CSV file.
