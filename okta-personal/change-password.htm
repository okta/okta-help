Reset your password

You can reset your Okta Personal password through the sign-in page. Password reset isn't available in the Settings menu during the beta release.

  1. In a desktop browser, go to personal.okta.com.

  2. On the Welcome page, enter your email address, and then click Continue.

  3. Click Forgot password?.

  4. On the Forgot Your Password? page, click Continue.

  5. Open the reset email, and then click Reset my password.

  6. Enter and confirm a new password, and then click Reset password.
