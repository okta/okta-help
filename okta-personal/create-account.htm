Create an account

Account registration is available through a desktop browser only during the beta release.

You can create an Okta Personal account with just an email address and a password. If you already use Okta for work, use a personal email address for Okta Personal.

  1. In a desktop browser, go to personal.okta.com.

  2. On the Welcome page, click Create account.

  3. Enter your email address, and then click Continue.

  4. Enter a password, and then click Continue.

  5. Open the confirmation email, and then click Verify Your Account.

  6. Select your security methods, or click Continue to skip this step for now. If you choose to wait, you're prompted for a password and a registration code each time you sign in.
