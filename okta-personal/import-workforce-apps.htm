Move apps to Okta Personal

After adding your Workforce account to the browser plugin, you can move apps to Okta Personal. Remember that only apps that use a personal email address can move to Okta Personal.

Before you begin

Link your accounts

Import apps

  1. In a desktop browser, go to your Okta Personal dashboard.

  2. Open the menu next to your name, and then select Import apps.

  3. On the source page, choose Okta workforce.

  4. Click Continue.

  5. Select your linked workforce account. If you haven’t linked an account yet, click Another account.

  6. When your eligible apps appear, select the ones you want to move to Okta Personal.

  7. Click Move apps.
