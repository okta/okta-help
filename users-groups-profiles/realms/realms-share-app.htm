Share an application with a realm

To share an application with a realm, you must assign the application to the group that will be associated with a realm.

Before you begin

  • Ensure that you’re signed to your Okta org as a super admin or a realm admin.
  • Complete the task listed in Get started with realms.

Start the task

Choose one of the following options:

  • Assign a single app to a group
  • Assign multiple apps to a group
