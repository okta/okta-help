Manage users

This is where you'll find the information you need to manage users in your org. Most of the tasks documented here are completed on the People page. To access the People page, go to Directory People . Here, you'll see a list of 200 users in increments of 25. You can filter the list to view users with a specific status and you can perform a number of actions including adding users, resetting passwords, resetting multifactor authentication, and more.
