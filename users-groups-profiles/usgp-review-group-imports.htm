Review group imports

After you configure provisioning on an app that supports groups, automatically imports groups from that app. Review the groups list to determine what groups were imported. After a successful import, scans for new users, new groups, or changes to existing user profiles or group memberships. If any of these are detected, automatically sends an email to the designated administrators that lists the number of users and groups scanned, added, updated, or removed during the import.
