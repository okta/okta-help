Monitor your tasks

The Tasks section lists issues in your org that require attention. You can filter by active (default) or unsubscribed tasks.

  • Type: Error, To-do, or Info (informational)

  • Item: The number of affected apps, users, or groups. This column is sortable to help you prioritize tasks.

  • Description: Summary of the task with linked details. See the following table.

  • Actions: Unsubscribe or Subscribe options for tracking, and a Mark all complete option for deprovisioning tasks. Tasks that you subscribed to are sorted as Active in the table filter.
