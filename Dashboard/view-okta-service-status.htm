View Okta service status

Use the Status section to view the real-time performance updates and service status of Okta for your cell. The information comes from the monitoring service at trust.okta.com.

There are three codes for the Okta service:

  • Operational: All Okta services are green.
  • Degradation: Issues have slowed Okta services or reduced their functionality.
  • Failed to load: The service status retrieval failed. Click Refresh page.
