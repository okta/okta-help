Monitor your apps

The Apps widget displays the number of successful user sign-ins across your org’s SAML/WS-Fed, OIDC, SWA, and other apps over time. The widget gives you a convenient overview of your org’s sign-in activity and helps you quickly identify org-wide trends and issues.

The Time period dropdown menu provides a list of time periods (Today, Yesterday, Last 7 days, and Last 30 days) that you can use to filter the widget.

You can click the View all apps link to go directly to the Applications page. You can also click the name of an app to view its integration settings.
