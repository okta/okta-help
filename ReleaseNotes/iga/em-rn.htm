Release: 2023.09.0

Features

  • Entitlement Management

    offers you a simple and a powerful way to ensure that users in an organization have the right permissions for each resource. The feature is integrated with and to help you manage and monitor users' access to resources, their level of access within these resources, and how the access was granted from the Okta Admin Console. Use to help meet your audit and compliance requirements for professional standards like SOC2, SOX, and others. See [%=okta-feature-names.Entitlement Management%].

    Note: Entitlement Management is a limited early access feature.
