Step 3: Connect the flows

  1. Return to the parent flow.

  2. Click Add Another > Function > List > For Each.

  3. Drag and drop the list field from the Flatten card to the list field in the For Each card.

  4. In the For Each card, click Choose Flow, and then select the helper flow that you just created.

  5. In the With the following values dropdown menus, choose id, type, and price.

  6. In the concurrency field, enter 3.

  7. Save the flow.
