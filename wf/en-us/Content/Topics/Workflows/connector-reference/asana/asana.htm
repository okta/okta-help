Asana connector

Organize and track your team's work using Asana.

  • Authorize your Asana account
  • Asana connector action cards

Authorize your Asana account

When you add an Asana card to a Flow for the first time, you'll be prompted to configure the connection. You'll be able to connect your Asana account, save your account information, and reuse the connection in new Flows that include Aaana. The Asana connector uses OAuth protocol. See Authorization.
