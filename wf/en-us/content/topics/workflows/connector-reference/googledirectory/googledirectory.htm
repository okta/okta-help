Google Workspace Admin connector

Google Workspace Admin allows you to manage Google Workspace for your organization. You can manage users and devices as well as configure security and settings from Google Workspace Admin.

  • Authorize your Google Workspace Admin account
  • Learn about Google Workspace Admin connector action cards

Authorize your Google Workspace Admin account

When you add a Google Workspace Admin card to a flow for the first time, you're prompted to create a connection. Connecting your account enables you to save your account information and reuse that connection the next time you build a flow with the connector. See Authorization.
