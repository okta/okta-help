Share diagnostic information with

When you share diagnostic and crash information with , your device sends information to about what's working and what's not working. You can stop sharing diagnostic information at any time.

  1. Click the gear icon.
  2. Select the Share information regarding crashes and diagnostics with Okta checkbox.

  3. Optional. Click Back to return to the Accounts screen.

Stop sharing diagnostic information with

  1. Click the gear icon.
  2. Clear the Share information regarding crashes and diagnostics with Okta checkbox.
  3. Optional. Click Back to return to the Accounts screen.
