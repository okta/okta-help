Disable on macOS devices

If your device supports verification and it is currently enabled, you can disable it for an account on your macOS device.

Before you begin

Be aware that you will not be able to disable user verification if your org requires it to remain enabled.

Make sure you are using a local device. Touch ID cannot be disabled in a remote session.

Start this task

  1. Open .

    2. For example, click the desktop shortcut, or if the app is running, from the menu bar extras, click the icon > Open.

  2. On the Accounts screen, click the account for which you want to disable .

  3. Click Disable .

    4. If the Disable button is not available: your organization requires user verification.

  4. Click Disable.

  5. As applicable, complete one of the following:
    • If your laptop lid is open, place a finger on the sensor when prompted, to disable .
    • If your laptop lid is closed (clamshell mode):
      1. In the new tab that opened, enter your username.

      2. Click Next.
      3. Enter your password.

      4. Click Verify.

        5. The Your identity has been verified message appears.

      5. Close the browser tab.

    The Account details screen appears with a message to indicate that was successfully disabled for the account.
