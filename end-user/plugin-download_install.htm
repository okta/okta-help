Install the

As an end user, you can download and install the for your browser.

Install the with Chrome

  1. In the Web Store, go to the page.
  2. Click Add to Chrome.
  3. Verify the permissions granted to the and then click Add Extension.

    If the isn’t visible in the task bar after adding the application, click the Extensions icon and then click Manage Extensions. Verify that the plugin is enabled. If it isn't active, turn on the toggle to enable the plugin.

  4. Sign in to the to complete the installation. Your org apps are visible in the pop-up window.

Install the Okta Browser Plugin with Safari

  1. In the Mac App Store, go to the page.
  2. Click View in Mac App Store.
  3. In the App Store, click Download.
  4. In the new window that opens, click Open Safari.
  5. In your toolbar, go to Safari Preferences Extensions .
  6. Select the Okta Extension App.
  7. Sign in to the to complete the installation. Your org app integrations are visible in the pop-up window.

Install the with Firefox

  1. In the Browser Add-Ons, go to the Okta Browser page.
  2. Click Add to Firefox.
  3. Verify the permissions granted to the , and then click Add Extension.
  4. Sign in to the to complete the installation. Your org app integrations are visible in the pop-up window.

Install the with Edge

  1. In the Add-ons store, go to the page.
  2. Click Get.
  3. Verify the permissions granted to the , and then click Add Extension.
  4. Sign in to the to complete the installation. Your org app integrations are visible through the pop-up window.

Install the with Internet Explorer

  1. Sign in to your .
  2. On the , click the Install the Plugin.
  3. If you're prompted to run or save the okta.swa.ie executable, select Run.
  4. In the installer, click Next on the Introduction step.
  5. Click Next on the Welcome step.
  6. Click Install.
  7. After the process is complete, click Finish.
  8. Go back your browser and follow the restart instructions in the new window.
  9. Sign in to the to complete the installation. Your org app integrations are visible through the pop-up window.
