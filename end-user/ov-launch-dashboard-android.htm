Before you begin

Ensure that the following conditions are met:

  • Okta FastPass is set up in . To check, open on your device and tap your account. On the Account details page, if is set up, it has a check mark.

  • You have the latest version of . For details, see Set up [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] on Android devices.

Start this task

  1. Open .
  2. Tap Launch dashboard.

    The browser opens.

  3. If prompted, choose a security method to confirm your identity, or enter your email address and tap Next.
  4. Confirm your identity.

    Enter your password or authenticate according to the security method that you selected.
