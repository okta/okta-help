Access apps from

Browse and use the applications from your home page.

Before you begin

To check if you use , open and tap your account. If has a green check mark, your organization uses the sign-in method.

Start this task

  1. Tap on your device to open the app. Your applications are displayed in a tile view, similar to your dashboard in the web browser on your computer.

  2. iOS: Optional. Change the display to a list of applications. Go to the Settings menu.

Considerations:

  • Android: As a security precaution, doesn't support downloading files or opening attachments from within . You can open files that don't require saving to disk.
  • iOS: From your dashboard, you can upload files from your device to any apps.
  • iOS: You can print the content of the browser to any AirPrint-enabled printer. Touch the printer button in the lower right corner to display the printing options.
