Change or reset your password

enables you to reset or change your password without having to contact your Help Desk. You can reset your password to be able to sign in to your apps protected by . After you sign in to the , you can change your password in Settings at any time.

Reset your password from the Sign-in Widget

To reset your password to a new one in case you've forgotten your password, do the following:

  1. On the Sign-in Widget, click Forgot password?. The Reset your password panel appears.

  2. In the Username field, enter your username, and click Next.

  3. Click Select for the security method you want to use to reset your password.

  4. The list of security methods may offer different options depending on how your org has been configured. These are some of the security methods you can choose from:

  • Email - When you select this option, you'll receive an email message that contains a link. When you click this link, your browser opens a page where you confirm that it's you who is signing in. After you confirm, click the original tab or window where you were signing in and follow the instructions that appear. You may be asked to answer a security question, if your org requires it. Enter a new password, and then the appears.

  • Get a push notification - To use this option, you must have the app installed and activated on your device. When you select this option, you'll receive a push notification in on your device. Tap Yes, it's me, and confirm your identity with biometrics, if prompted. You'll return to the browser window where you were signing in; follow the instructions that appear. You may be asked to answer a security question, if your org requires it. Enter a new password, and then the appears.

  • Phone (SMS / Voice Call) - You can use this option if your organization has enabled it, and if you provided a phone number on the My Settings page. When you select this option, you'll receive either a phone call or an SMS message on your phone. Enter the number you hear or receive into the Sign-in Widget and click Next. You may be asked to answer a security question, if your org requires it. Enter a new password, and then the appears.

Change your password in Settings

After you log in to the , you can change your password any time you wish.

  1. On the , click the arrow beside your name in the menu bar and select Settings. The Account page appears.

  2. Click Edit Profile if it appears, and then authenticate with a security method, if required.

  3. In the Change Password section, enter your current password in the Current password field.

  4. In the New password and Confirm new password fields, enter your new password.

  5. Click Change Password.
