considerations

Learn about notices and exceptions.

To check if you use , open and tap your account. If has a green checkmark, your organization uses the sign-in method.

  • You must authenticate after prolonged inactivity. If you haven't used for more than 30 days, you are prompted to enter your credentials when you eventually open . This occurs because relies on an internal token for authentication that expires after 30 days of inactivity. This token expiration is separate from PIN and MFA expiration occurrences.
  • On mobile devices, some apps require you to authenticate with the app itself. provides one-click access to most supported apps. After signing in to , you can access the apps on your device without having to authenticate with the app itself. Many apps provide a fallback authentication method so that you can access the app by entering your app sign-in credentials. Some apps still require you to enter your app credentials when you access them on your mobile device. If you encounter problems, contact your system administrator.
  • Apps in open directly in the browser. If your administrator didn't enable Mobility Management (OMM) for your organization, apps in open directly in the browser when you long-press an app.
  • On Android devices, you can't download files from an web browser. As a security precaution, doesn't support downloading files or opening attachments from an web browser on Android devices. This restriction minimizes malware threats. There are no restriction on opening files that don't require saving to disk.
  • You can rate on iOS devices. On iOS devices, you are prompted to rate the app in the App Store. After clicking Submit, the page opens in the App Store so that you can provide additional feedback. To dismiss this option, click Not now.
  • On Android 12, some features don’t work if Okta Mobile is set in restrictive mode.
  • If you use Okta Mobile 4.21.0 on Android 11 or 12, you might not be able to access Play for Work. This message appears: “Play for Work is not available. Play for Work is still being enabled, please try again later.” To resolve this issue, set up your work profile again.
