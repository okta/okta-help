On the Account Details page, you can check what security methods are set up for your account.

When you sign in to your app accounts protected by , you can confirm your identity with in different ways. To find out what authentication options are available to you, open and tap your account. On the Account Details page, the enabled security methods have a checkmark.

In this example, you can sign in with Okta Verify in three ways: 

  • Authentication Code: You use this option when you select Okta Verify Or enter code or Enter a code on the sign-in page.
  • Push Notification: You use this option when you select Okta Verify Send Push or Get a push notification on the sign-in page.
  • Okta FastPass*: You use this option when you select Use or Sign in with on the sign-in page. With this option, you don’t have to provide a password to access your account securely.
    *

Enable Face ID or Touch ID

If you didn’t enable Face Id or Touch ID during the setup, and biometric verification is required in your organization, you’re prompted to enable this option.

If you change the Face ID or Touch ID settings on your device, you go out of sync with the Okta Verify settings. If prompted, sync or enable Face ID or Touch ID.

  1. Open and tap your account.
  2. On the Account Details page, go to the Options section and enable Face ID or Touch ID.

  3. If prompted, go to the Settings menu on your phone to configure Face ID or Touch ID. Then, return to to set up this type of verification in the app.
  4. If prompted, verify your identity. Tap Sign in.
  5. Enter your organization's email address. This is the email where you received the setup instructions from your administrator. The Setup Complete screen indicates what sign-in methods were enabled.
  6. Tap Done to finalize the procedure. The Face ID or Touch ID toggle is now on.

Disable Face ID or Touch ID

If Face ID or Touch ID is mandatory in your organization, you can’t disable this verification method.

  1. Open and tap your account.
  2. On the Account Details page, go to the Options section and turn off Face ID or Touch ID.

  3. When prompted, confirm your identity by scanning your fingerprint or face.
  4. After you confirm your identity, Face ID or Touch ID is disabled.
