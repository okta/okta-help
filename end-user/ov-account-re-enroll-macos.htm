Re-enroll an account on macOS devices

You can re-enroll your account if your admin has reset your authenticator.

  1. On the Account details screen, click Re-enroll.

    2. If you don't have the option to re-enroll, contact your help desk for additional support.

  2. Complete steps 10 to 12 of Add an account to [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] by using a sign-in URL on macOS devices.
