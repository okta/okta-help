Sign in with on iOS devices

If the sign-in window prompts you to confirm your identity with , and you select this option, you can access your app accounts securely without having to enter a password.

Before you begin

Ensure that the latest version of is installed on your device.

Start this task

When you access an app or the on your device, you might be prompted to authenticate with :

When you tap Use Okta FastPass or Sign in with Okta FastPass, checks your identity.

Confirm your identity as prompted.

  • If you enabled Face ID or Touch ID in your account, prompts you for Face ID or Touch ID. Scan your face or fingerprint.
  • Otherwise, tap the consent window.

When you confirm your identity, you gain access to your app account or the apps dashboard.

If requires more configurations, follow the messages on your device.

  • If prompted to enable Face ID or Touch ID, tap Enable.
  • If extra setup is required for , tap Set Up.

  • If you don’t have an account yet, tap Add Account when prompted and follow the instructions.

Known issues

  • When you sign in to an app with on an iPad or iOS device using Chrome or Safari, Okta might ask you to download although you already have it installed. If this issue occurs, clear the browser cache and access your app again. When prompted to open the app, tap Yes. If you tap No, the browser ignores the link to the app until you clear the cache again. If the issue persists, update your device operating system to the latest version.
