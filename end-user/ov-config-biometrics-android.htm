Configure biometrics in your account on Android devices

In some organizations, you can set up biometrics in as an extra way to confirm your identity when you sign in to apps protected by .

Tasks

  • Enable biometrics
  • Disable biometrics

Enable biometrics

Before you enable biometrics in , set up biometrics in your device settings. If you set up a work profile on your device to separate work and personal apps, might be installed in your work profile. In this case, set up biometrics in your work profile in addition to the settings you already made on your device. Depending on the device manufacturer, the work profile biometric settings page is under Settings Security or Settings Work profile . See your device documentation for details.

If you didn’t enable biometrics during the account setup, you're prompted to do so later. Follow the prompts in the app.

If you change the biometric settings on your device, you go out of sync with the settings. If prompted, sync or enable biometrics.

  1. Open and tap your account.
  2. On the Account details page, go to the Options section and turn on Use biometrics.

  3. If prompted, go to your device Settings to configure biometrics. Then, return to to set it up in the app.
  4. If prompted, confirm your identity. Tap Sign in.
  5. Enter your organization's email address. This is the email where you received the setup instructions from your technology department.

    The Setup Complete screen indicates what authentication methods you enabled.

  6. Tap Done to complete the procedure. The biometrics toggle is now on.

Disable biometrics

If biometric verification is mandatory in your organization, you can’t disable it.

  1. Open and tap your account.
  2. On the Account details page, go to the Options section and turn off Use biometrics.

  3. When prompted, verify your identity with biometrics.

The biometric verification option is now disabled.
