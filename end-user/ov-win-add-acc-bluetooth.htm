Add your account to another Windows device by Bluetooth

If you already use and want to authenticate with the same account on another Windows device, you can sync your devices using Bluetooth. By doing so, you can add your existing account directly to the other device without signing in to the End-User Dashboard.

Before you begin

  • You must have biometrics enabled on your new device. Check the settings app to confirm.

  • Confirm that both your new and old device has Bluetooth and it’s turned on.

  • Open on your old device and confirm that your account doesn’t have any warnings or errors. See Resolve [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] account issues on Android devices or Resolve [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] account issues on iOS devices.

Start this task

  1. Start on the device where you already have an account. Open and generate a code that you need to set up your new device. Follow the steps for your device type:

    A QR code and a code with numbers and letters appear. Keep them handy. You're prompted for the code later.

  2. Install on your new Windows device.

  3. Open the app, and then go to Add account from another device.

  4. Click Add account.

  5. To allow Okta Verify to add your account from another device by using Bluetooth connection, click Allow.

  6. Set up the account. For this step, you need the code generated by in the first step. Enter the 8-digit code that is displayed on the other device. If the account setup is successful, a 6-digit PIN appears.

  7. Confirm that you're pairing the right devices. Enter the 6-digit PIN displayed in Okta Verify on the new device into on your other device. If the PIN is correct, you receive a confirmation message.

    Don't enter a PIN given to you by anyone else.

  8. On the new device, follow the instructions to complete the account setup.

  9. If your account setup is successful, you receive a confirmation message in on your new device.

You can now sign in with on the new device.
