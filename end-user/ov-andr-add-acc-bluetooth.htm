Add your account to another Android device by Bluetooth

If you already use and want to authenticate with the same account on another Android device, you can sync your devices using Bluetooth. By doing so, you can add your existing account directly to the other device without signing in to the End-User Dashboard.

Before you begin

  • Use the latest version of . Check the App Store for updates.

  • You must have enabled in your Okta Verify account.

  • You must have biometrics enabled on your new device. Check the settings app to confirm.

  • Confirm that both your new and old device has Bluetooth and it’s turned on.

  • Open on your old device and confirm that your account doesn’t have any warnings or errors. See Resolve [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] account issues on Android devices or Resolve [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] account issues on iOS devices.

  • If your device has Android 11 or an older version, turn on the location in your phone's settings. See Android documentation.

    doesn't collect or use the device location. needs the location to sync the two devices by Bluetooth. For details see Android documentation.

Start this task

  1. Start on the device where you already have an account. Open and generate a code that you need to set up your new device. Follow the steps for your device type:

    A QR code and a code with numbers and letters appear. Keep them handy. You need the code later.

  2. Install on your new Android device.

  3. Open the app, and then tap Add account from another device.

  4. To allow Okta Verify to add your account from another device by using Bluetooth connection, tap Allow. If Bluetooth isn’t enabled yet, can turn it on for you if you grant permission. If you receive the An app wants to turn on Bluetooth message, tap Allow.

  5. Set up the account. For this step, you need the code generated by in the first step.

    • Scan the QR code displayed on your original device. Tap Scan QR code, and then point your camera at the QR code displayed on another device.

    • If your device can't scan the QR code, tap Enter Code Manually. Enter the 8-digit code that is displayed in the browser on another device.

      • If the account setup is successful, a 6-digit PIN appears.

  6. Confirm that you're pairing the right devices. Enter the 6-digit PIN displayed in Okta Verify on the new device into on your other device.

    Don't enter a PIN given to you by anyone else.

  7. To enable push notifications or biometrics on the new device, grant your permission if prompted. If your account setup is successful, you receive a confirmation message.

  8. Tap Done to complete the account setup.

You can now sign in with on your new device.
