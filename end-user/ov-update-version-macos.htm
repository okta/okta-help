Update your macOS version

  • If your device is managed by your organization, your organization might have a policy that will restrict you from updating your macOS.

  • If you update your macOS version, also update your Okta Verify app.

You can use the Software Update application on your macOS device to update your macOS to the latest version.

Before you begin

  • Identify your current macOS version.

    • See Find out which macOS your Mac is using .

  • Verify that your device meets the model compatibility and disk space requirements.

    • To check how much disk space your device has, go to the Apple menu > About This Mac > Storage.

    See Apple’s website for specific macOS system requirements, for example, macOS Big Sur - Technical Specifications.

  • Back up your macOS

    • See Back up your Mac with Time Machine.

  • Make sure your internet connection is working.

Procedure

To update your macOS to the latest version, see Update macOS on Mac.
