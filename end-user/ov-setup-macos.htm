Get started with on macOS devices

To authenticate with Okta Verify on your desktop, you must install the app and set up an account.

Topics

  • Set up [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] on your macOS device by using a sign-in URL
  • Set up [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] on your macOS device while accessing an app

Before you begin

Obtain your organization’s sign-in URL. This might be available in the Welcome/Activation email that you received from your administrator.

Start this task

  1. If your IT department did not already install on your device, go to the Apple App Store, download , and then install it.
  2. Open .

    3. For example, click the desktop shortcut, or if the app is running, from the menu bar extras, click the icon > Open.

  3. Click Add Account.
  4. Enter your organization’s sign-in URL.

    5. Your sign-in URL is the web address you use in your browser to access your company’s portal. It might also be available in the Welcome/Activation email that you received from your administrator.

  5. Click Next.

    6. The Check your browser screen appears, and a browser window opens.

  6. In the browser window, complete the following in the :
    1. Enter your email address, and then click Next.
    2. Enter your password, and then click Verify.
  7. In the Touch ID with Okta Verify window, click Next.

  8. If your fingerprint isn’t yet set up for Touch ID, Okta Verify prompts you to add your fingerprint. Click Add fingerprint. Follow the instructions and click Done. Then, click I’ve added a fingerprint.

  9. Click Enable Touch ID. This page appears if your fingerprint is already set up for Touch ID on your device.

  10. Scan your finger on the fingerprint reader.

    11. A message confirms that your account was added successfully.

    You can now use to authenticate when you access apps.
