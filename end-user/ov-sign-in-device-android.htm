Sign in with Okta FastPass on Android devices

If you sign in with , you can access your app accounts securely without a password.

Before you begin

  • Ensure that you have the latest version.

  • If your browser is Firefox browser, go to Settings and change the Open links in apps option to Always.

Sign in with

You can use to sign in to native mobile apps, app links, or the . uses your account to check your identity.

  1. Tap Use Okta FastPass or Sign in with Okta FastPass.
  2. Confirm your identity. Scan your fingerprint or tap the consent window.

If Okta confirms your identity, you gain access to your app account or the apps dashboard.

Configure to use

If you don’t have an account yet, or if your account needs extra setup, opens and provides setup details.

  • To add an account, tap Add account.

  • To configure your account for , tap Set up.

When the setup is complete, you can use to access your apps protected by .

Known issues

  • In the case of some Android native applications, when you try to sign in to your account with , doesn’t open. Instead, you receive the message Additional setup required to use Okta FastPass along with setup instructions. These native apps don’t support yet. Use a different security method to access your app account.
