Resolve account issues on Android devices

Learn how to resolve account problems.

If there is an issue with your account, a warning appears on the accounts page:

Tap the account to find out how to resolve the problem. One of these issues might have occurred:

  • Biometrics required
  • Biometrics are out of sync
  • MFA reset issues
  • Your account expired

Biometrics required

If your company requires you to use biometrics to confirm your identity, the Biometrics required message appears:

  1. Tap Enable.
  2. If prompted, configure biometrics from the device Settings menu and then return to to set it up in the app. Otherwise, confirm your identity with biometrics.
  3. To proceed with identity verification, tap Sign in.
  4. Select Password or any other security methods from the sign-in page.
  5. Enter your password or confirm your identity according to the security method you selected.

Biometrics are now enabled in . You can scan your face or fingerprint to confirm your identity.

Biometrics are out of sync

If the biometric settings on your device are no longer in sync with , the Sync biometrics message appears:

  1. Tap Sync biometrics.
  2. Confirm your identity with biometrics.
  3. To proceed with identity verification, tap Sign in.
  4. Select Password or any other security method from the sign-in page.
  5. Enter your password or confirm your identity according to the security method you selected.

confirms that the biometrics setup is successful.

MFA reset issues

A warning might appear when you try to sign in to an app with . You are asked to re-enroll your account. To resolve the problem, open and tap your account.

If multifactor authentication was reset for the device, the MFA reset message appears:


  1. Tap Re-enroll.
  2. Confirm your identity with biometrics if prompted.
  3. To proceed with identity verification, tap Sign in.
  4. If prompted, enter your email address and tap Next.

    This is the email address that you used when you set up your account for the first time.

    The email address might already be filled out for you.

  5. Select Password or any other security method from the sign-in page.
  6. Enter your password or confirm your identity according to the security method you selected.

A message confirms that your account is re-enrolled. You can now use to sign in to your apps protected by .
