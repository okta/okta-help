Sign in with an Okta Verify push notification (Android)

To authenticate with a push notification, select this option in the sign-in window and approve the notification sent to your device. For enhanced security, your organization might present you with a number matching request.

Before you begin

Ensure that you have the latest version of . Go to Google Play Store – Okta Verify and install the latest version if needed.

Start this task

  1. Select the push notification option in the sign-in window.
  2. Check your device for a push notification from .

    The notification expires after five minutes if you don't approve or reject the verification request. To receive a new notification on your device, go back to the app you want to access, and select the push notification option again.

  3. Confirm your identity.
    •  If prompted, tap Yes, it's me or tap Review. The push notification specifies what application you’re trying to access.

      If prompted, tap the number that matches what you see in the sign-in window.

    •  If detects an unusual sign-in attempt, asks you to review the sign-in details. considers a sign-in attempt to be unusual based on various reasons, including details about your device and location.

      Tap Review.

      Check the flagged location or device in the app.

      • If the details match your device and current location, tap Yes or tap the number that matches what you see on the sign-in page.

      • If you don’t recognize your device or location, tap No, it’s not me or Cancel the sign-in attempt. This action cancels the authentication attempt and prevents access to the -protected account.

  4. Confirm your identity with biometrics if prompted.

If your identity is confirmed, you gain access to your app account.
