Get started with Okta Verify on Windows devices

To authenticate with Okta Verify on your desktop, you must install the app and set up an account.

Before you begin

Obtain your organization’s sign-in URL. This might be available in a Welcome/Activation email that you received from your administrator.

Start this task

  1. If your IT department didn't already install on your device, install the app when prompted. If the app isn't available on your device contact your administrator.
  2. Click Get startedAdd account.
  3. Enter your organization’s sign-in URL. This URL is the web address that you use in your browser to access your company’s portal. It might also be available in a Welcome/Activation email that you received from your administrator.
  4. Click Next. A browser window opens.
  5. Enter your credentials in the sign-in window:
    1. Enter your email address, and then click Next.
    2. Enter your password, and then click Verify.
  6. As applicable, complete one of the following steps:
    • If biometric authentication is required in your organization and your device supports biometrics, enable Windows Hello in . Click Enable. Then, scan your finger on the fingerprint reader.
    • If Windows Hello isn't yet set up on your device, follow the instructions to set it up. Then scan your finger on the fingerprint reader.
    • If biometric authentication isn't required in your organization, you can skip Windows Hello enablement.

    A message confirms that your account was added successfully.

    When you access apps, you can now authenticate with Okta Verify.
