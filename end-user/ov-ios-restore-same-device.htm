Restore on the same iOS device

If you reset your device, deleted , or deleted your account from the app, you might be able to restore your account so that you can authenticate with the app again. However, it might not be enough to just install the app again. You might have to first remove your existing enrollment from the Settings page on your organization’s . Some restrictions apply.

This topic is for users who already had an account on the device. If you’re a new user and have not yet set up , see Set up [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] on iOS devices.

Before you begin

Start this procedure

  • Task 1. In a browser, remove Okta Verify from your account settings.
  • Task 2. Install [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] and add your account.

Task 1. In a browser, remove Okta Verify from your account settings.

Task 2. Install and add your account.

  1. Go to your device. If you don’t have , download it from the Apple App Store and install it.
  2. Open the app to add your account again.
    • If you just installed , tap Add account.
    • If you already had on your device, tap Add (+).
  3. Choose the type of account to add. Tap Organization.
  4. Choose your account setup method.
    • Ensure the code is shown on another device such as a laptop or desktop. Tap Yes, Ready To Scan, and point your camera at the QR code displayed in the browser on another device.
    • If you don’t know how to obtain the QR code, tap Where do I get my QR code?
    • If you can’t scan the QR code, tap No, Sign In Instead. Follow the instructions in Set up [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] on your iOS device by signing in to your account.
  5. If prompted, allow push notifications on your device so that you can approve future sign-in notifications without opening the app.
  6. If prompted, enable Face ID or Touch ID. Tap Enable. A message confirms that your account was added.
  7. If the following screen appears on your browser, click Finish. (This screen appears only in some cases).

  8. Tap Done to complete the account enrollment. Your new account is now available in the app.
