Install

Install and sign in to to access your applications on your device.

Before you begin

  • Check if you use . Open and tap your account. If has a green checkmark, your organization uses the sign-in method.

  • Search for in the Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). If Google Play Store is not available for you, contact your Help Desk.

On Android 12, some features don’t work if Okta Mobile is set in restrictive mode.

Start this task

  1. Install on your device.
  2. Open the app.
  3. Sign in by entering your company's sign-in URL (Site name) and your credentials (username and password). Site name is your sign-in URL. For example, if your company's domain name is acme.com, your sign-in URL (site name) might be acme.okta.com.

    When signing in to for the first time, ensure that you enter the correct site name to avoid errors. A network error might occur if you enter your company’s domain name (for example, acme.com) or your email address (for example, yourname@acme.com) instead of the correct site name (for example, acme.okta.com).


    Don't know your username? Your username is created for you by your admin. Most new users receive a Welcome email from the IT department which contains their username and a link allowing them to create a password and activate their account. If you didn't receive a Welcome email, contact your administrator.
  4. Optional. Complete the authentication step as prompted by .If multifactor authentication (MFA) is enabled for your organization, you are prompted to an additional authentication step to sign in.
    If your organization uses Okta Mobility Management, you are prompted to enroll your device.
