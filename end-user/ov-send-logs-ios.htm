Send feedback from your iOS device

Use the Send Feedback menu to share suggestions or ideas about , or to report issues that you might encounter. To send feedback from anywhere in the app, shake your device.

Start this task

  1. Open on your iOS device and tap the menu (...) in the top-right corner.
  2. Tap Send Feedback and follow the instructions.

    You can also send feedback from the Settings page.


From the Settings page you can send and clear logs. Before sending logs, ensure that the Apple Mail app is available and set up on your device. If you don't have the Mail app on your device, download it from the Apple App Store, install it, and create an account.
The Mail app is not required for the Send Feedback option.

  • To send logs, tap Diagnostics Send Logs .
  • To clear logs, tap Diagnostics Clear Logs .
