Leave the beta program

If you want to use the app’s production version, you can leave the beta program.

  1. Open your beta app.
  2. From the menu in the top-right corner, tap Settings Join beta .
  3. Scroll down and tap Leave the program.
  4. Uninstall the beta app and then install the public version from Google Play Store.
