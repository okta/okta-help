Share diagnostic information with from your macOS device

You can help improve , by sharing your diagnostic and crash information with . This information is anonymous, it does not include any personal information.

Share diagnostic information with

When you share diagnostic and crash information with , your device sends information to about what's working and what's not working. You can stop sharing diagnostic information at any time.

If you are adding a new account to , select the Automatically send crash reports to checkbox on the How it works screen to share diagnostic information with .

  1. Open .

    2. For example, click the desktop shortcut, or if the app is running, from the menu bar extras, click the icon > Open.

  2. Click the gear icon.
  3. Select the Automatically send crash reports to Okta checkbox.

    4. If your org has manually set reporting settings, this option is not available for selection.

  4. Optional. Click Back to return to the Accounts screen.

Stop sharing diagnostic information with

  1. Open .

    2. For example, click the desktop shortcut, or if the app is running, from the menu bar extras, click the icon > Open.

  2. Click the gear icon.
  3. Clear the Share information regarding crashes and diagnostics with Okta checkbox.

    4. If your org requires information, this option is not available for selection.

  4. Optional. Click Back to return to the Accounts screen.
