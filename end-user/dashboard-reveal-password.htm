View your Okta passwords

On the , you can reveal and copy your passwords for apps that have been added to your dashboard.

  1. Sign in to . In your browser on your computer type your sign-in URL, such as https://org-name.okta.com, where org-name is the name of your company or organization. Enter your username and password and follow the authentication prompts.
  2. From the Home page, click the ellipsis icon on an app card to view its details.
  3. In the new panel that opens, click the See Password to open a drop-down list containing your username and password. Your password will still be hidden, but your username will be listed.
  4. Click Reveal Password to view your password for that application or click Copy Password to copy it to your clipboard.
  5. You may be asked to reauthenticate when trying to copy or reveal your password.

Passwords for apps assigned by your org may only be viewed and copied if your admin has enabled this feature for that app. If admins disable the ability to view passwords for an org-managed app, access to the app is possible if the is installed on your browser.
