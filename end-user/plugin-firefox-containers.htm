Configure containers

The Multi-Account Containers extension allows you to minimize distractions and categorize the websites you use while browsing. Use the following steps to enable the to work with containers, allowing you keep your SWA apps organized within your browser.

Prerequisites

Ensure that you’ve added the Multi-Account Containers extension to your browser before configuring the .

Procedure

  1. Click the Containers icon on your browser toolbar.

  2. Select a container that you want to assign to . creates a tab within that container.
  3. Go to your and click the Containers icon again.
  4. Select Always open in to assign the to that container.

  5. When prompted, confirm your container assignment by selecting Remember my decision for this site.

    If you don’t select Remember my decision for this site, the browser prompts you to decide each time you launch a SWA application.

  6. Click Open in Current Container.

Limitations

Going to a SWA app directly
If a user goes directly to an -managed application, either through a bookmarked link or a web search, doesn't categorize that website into a container.
