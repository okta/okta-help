Configure Windows Hello or passcode verification in on Windows devices

If your device supports Windows Hello or device passcode verification, you might be prompted to enable this feature in your account.

Tasks

  • Enable Windows Hello or device passcode verification
  • Disable Windows Hello or device passcode verification

Enable Windows Hello or device passcode verification

  1. In the Accounts window, click your account.
  2. Click the toggle to turn on Windows Hello and follow the instructions.

If your organization requires you to use Windows Hello, prompts you to enable it.

If Windows Hello in is out of sync with your device settings, click Sync when the message appears.

Disable Windows Hello or device passcode verification

  1. In the Accounts window, click your account.
  2. Click the toggle to turn off Windows Hello and follow the instructions.

A message confirms that you disabled Windows Hello or device passcode verification.
