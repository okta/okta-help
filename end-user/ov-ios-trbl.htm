Can’t add my existing account to a new or existing device

Issue

  • I have a new device, but I can’t add my existing account.
  • I lost my device. How do I install on my new device?
  • I set up already but deleted the app from my device.
  • I had to factory reset my device.

Solution

Your account might still be tied to your original app installation. Installing the app on the new device or reinstalling the app on your current device and adding the account back might not be enough. These are some possible solutions:

  • Sign in to your organization’s and tap the arrow next to your username. Go to Settings and remove . Then, set up again. See Restore [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] on the same iOS device.
  • If you lost your original device, sign in to your with a different security method. See Sign in to resources protected by [%=okta-variables.ProductName%].
  • If is your only security sign-in method and you can’t access your account, contact your help desk.

Can’t sign in with push notifications

Issue

  • The sign-in page tells me to check , but the app doesn't receive the push notification.
  • I can't sign in to my account even if I approve the push notification sent to my device.

Solution

Ensure that these conditions are met:

  • In on your device, you have an account for your organization. This organization provides the app that you are trying to sign in to.
  • notifications are turned on.
  • The date and time on your device are set to automatic. The clock on your device must be in sync with the time maintained by your cellular provider’s network. Small differences between your device clock and the network clock can cause issues.
  • The latest version of is installed on your device. For details, see Set up [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] on iOS devices.
  • See Troubleshoot missing [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] push notifications on iOS devices.

If your problem persists, contact your IT admin or help desk, or restore your account. See Restore [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] on the same iOS device.

I don’t know what to do with the code from

The app generates a code that you enter in the sign-in window if you select this sign-in option.

You don’t have to use the code to sign in with Okta Verify. You can also sign in to apps with push notifications if your organization configured this option.

Okta Verify keeps generating a code although I’m not looking for a code to sign in

The six-digit code is one way to authenticate. The app generates this code every 30 seconds when you open it. After you close , the code is no longer generated. The performance of your phone is not affected.

You need the code only if you selected the code option in the authentication window. If you authenticate with push notifications, you don’t need this code.
