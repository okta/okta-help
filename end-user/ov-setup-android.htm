is an app that helps protect your data, ensuring that only you can access your app accounts.

When you access apps protected by , you select a security method to confirm your identity. If your organization uses , you can select any of these security methods as long as it was configured by your admin:

  • A push notification sent to your device
  • A temporary six-digit code

To set up on your Android device for the first time, go to your computer and open your organization’s Welcome or Multifactor Authentication (MFA) activation email. Click the sign-in URL to access your organization's . Follow the instructions to obtain a QR code. Then, download and install on your device, and scan the QR code displayed on the computer. You can also set up without a QR code.

Before you begin

  • Check and confirm that you are using the correct software versions. See Supported platforms, browsers, and operating systems.
  • Check if QR code scanning is enabled on your device. A QR code is a universal barcode that your device camera can scan and read. uses QR codes to easily register your device when you set up .

    The process for scanning QR codes depends on the device manufacturer. Check the instructions for enabling QR code scanning on your device.

  • Open the Welcome or MFA activation email from your administrator. This email includes a link to your organization’s sign-in URL and your username (typically, it’s your email address). You need this information to set up your account. If you didn’t receive an email with instructions, contact your help desk.

Before you set up on Google Pixel 4 XL, ensure that you enabled biometrics on your device. During the account setup, might prompt you to confirm your identity with biometrics.
