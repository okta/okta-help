Sign in with on Android devices

If your organization uses to ensure that you access your app accounts securely, you are prompted to confirm your identity with an security method when you try to access your apps.

The following security methods that appear on the sign-in page use : 

  • Enter a code
  • Get a push notification
  • Use

To check which security methods you can use with your account, open on your device and tap your account. On the Account details page, the security methods that have a check mark are enabled. This means that you can use any of these methods to confirm your identity when you sign in to apps protected by Okta.

  • Authentication code: If you select Enter a code on the sign-in page, generates a code. You enter this code on the sign-in page to confirm your identity.
  • Push notification: If you select Get a push notification on the sign-in page, an message pops up on your device. Tap Yes to confirm your identity.
  • Okta FastPass*: If you choose Use or Sign in with Okta FastPass on the sign-in page, you use this authentication option. You don’t have to provide a password to access your account securely.
    *

Before you begin

  • Ensure that the latest version of is installed on your device. Open the app to confirm that your account is set up. For details, see Set up [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] on Android devices.
  • Ensure that is not restricted from running in the background. Go to Settings Battery Adaptive Battery Restricted apps and confirm that is not listed as a restricted app. If is listed on this page, clear the check box to allow the app to run in the background.
