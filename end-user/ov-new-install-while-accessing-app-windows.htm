Add an account to by accessing an -protected app on Windows devices

On Windows devices, you can add an account to by accessing an app on the .

Before you begin

  • Confirm that you're using the correct software versions. See Supported platforms, browsers, and operating systems.
  • Open the Welcome/Activation email from your organization. This email includes a link to Your organization's sign-in page and Your username (typically, it's your email address). You need this information to set up your account. If you didn't receive the email, contact your company’s Help desk.

Start this task

  1. Complete one of the following:
    • In the , click an app.
    • In the , right-click an app and select Copy Link Address, and then paste the URL in a browser.
  2. Click Sign in with .
  3. In the Open ? pop-up, click Open .

  4. As applicable, complete one of the following:
    • If this is your first account with :
      1. Click Get started.

      2. Click Next.

    • If this is not your first account with , click Add account.

    The Check your browser screen appears.

  5. In the new tab that opened, enter your Username.

  6. Click Next.
  7. Enter your Password.

  8. Click Verify.

    9. A Your identity has been verified message displays.

  9. Close the browser tab.
  10. As applicable, complete one of the following:

    • If your device supports Windows Hello, Windows Hello is not currently configured on your device, and your organization gives you the option of using it: click Set up, and then follow the screen prompts to set up Windows Hello; or, click Not now, to set it up later.

    • If your device supports Windows Hello and your organization requires it, click Set up, and then place a finger on the Windows Hello sensor when prompted to set up Windows Hello.

    • If your device does not support Windows Hello and your organization requires it, click Got it. You will not be able to add an account. Contact your admin.

  11. As applicable, complete one of the following:

    • If Windows Hello is optional, click Enable to enable Windows Hello, or Not now to skip the Windows Hello setup.

    • If Windows Hello is required, click Enable to enable Windows Hello.

  12. If this is not your first account, click Set default to set this account as your default account, or click Skip. You can go to your Account Details screen at any time to make changes to the default account.

    13. The Account added screen verifies that your account was successfully added to , and then the Accounts screen displays.

    The Accounts screen lists all the accounts that are set up in your app.
