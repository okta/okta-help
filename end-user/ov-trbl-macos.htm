Troubleshoot on macOS devices

Learn how to troubleshoot problems on macOS devices and how to report issues.

If your problem persists, contact your help desk.

  • How do I report issues?

    • In the menu bar extras, go to > Report Issue. Provide the required information and submit your report. Logged data is included automatically. This data is anonymous and can help Okta troubleshoot your problem.

    See Share diagnostic information with [%=okta-variables.ProductName%] from your macOS device.

  • Can I install on multiple devices and add the same accounts to each installation?

    • Yes. If you want to install on multiple mobile and desktop devices, first install on your mobile device (iOS or Android) and set up multiple authentication factors (for example, Yubikey or SMS), and then install on your macOS device.

  • My Account Details screen has a “Your device or account was invalidated for use on ” error. How do I fix this?

    • Your account is no longer valid, so it can no longer be used. If you have the option to re-enroll, re-enroll your account. Otherwise, contact your help desk if you need additional support.

    See Re-enroll an [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] account on macOS devices.

  • How do I disable ?

    • See Disable [%=3rd Party Integrations.Touch ID (Apple)%] on macOS devices. Note that if is required by your organization, you can’t disable it. Also, if your app requires , it might not be accessible if is disabled.

  • Why does the Setup screen appear when I use a Magic Keyboard with for authentication?

    • This is a known issue. It occurs if the Magic Keyboard with disconnects from your desktop device during enrollment or authentication. assumes that is not set up on the device, so it prompts you to enable . To avoid this issue, make sure the Magic Keyboard is correctly connected to the desktop device, the sensor is accessible, and that the hardware works as expected.

  • I changed the setting on my device. Now, the Sign in with option still appears in the list of authenticators, but it’s not working. How can I fix this?

    • Disable in , and then enable it again.

    See Disable [%=3rd Party Integrations.Touch ID (Apple)%] on macOS devices, and Enable or update [%=3rd Party Integrations.Touch ID (Apple)%] on macOS devices.

  • Sometimes I’m not prompted for when I try to access a restricted resource through . Why?

    • This a known issue for macOS 11 (Big Sur) and earlier, but Apple has fixed the issue for macOS 12 (Monterey). You can avoid this issue in the future by updating your device to macOS 12 (Monterey). To resolve this issue now, restart .

    See Re-start [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] on macOS devices.

  • I was locked out of , how do I enable it again?

    • See Unlock [%=3rd Party Integrations.Touch ID (Apple)%] on your macOS device.

  • I am unable to access the Google Drive File Stream native app. Why?

    • This is a known issue. As a workaround, click the Sign in with your browser instead link in the lower portion of the page to access the app.

  • How do I delete from my device?

    • See Delete the [%=okta-feature-names.Okta Verify%] app from a macOS device.

  • I deleted from my device. Can I reinstall it and set up myself without contacting the help desk?

    • If you are able to, install from the Apple App Store and then add an account to register your macOS device. If you don't have an Apple ID or if your laptop is managed by your organization, contact your help desk.
