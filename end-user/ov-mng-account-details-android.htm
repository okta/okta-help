See account details on Android devices

On the Account details page, you can check what authentication options are set up for your account.

When you sign in to your app accounts protected by , you can confirm your identity with in different ways. To find out what authentication options are available to you, open and tap your account. On the Account details page, the enabled security methods have a checkmark.

In this example, you can sign in with in three ways: 

  • Authentication Code: You use this option when you select Okta Verify Or enter code or Enter a code on the sign-in page.
  • Push Notification: You use this option when you select Okta Verify Send Push or Get a push notification on the sign-in page.
  • Okta FastPass*: You use this option when you select Use or Sign in with on the sign-in page. With this option, you don’t have to provide a password to access your account securely.
    *

Enable biometrics

If you didn’t enable biometrics during the setup, you might be prompted to do so later. Follow the prompts in the app.

  1. Open and tap your account.
  2. On the Account details page, go to the Options section and turn on Use biometrics.

  3. If prompted, configure biometrics from the device Settings menu and then return to to set it up in the app.
  4. If prompted, verify your identity. Tap Sign in.
  5. Enter your organization's email address. This is the email where you received the setup instructions from your organization.
    The Setup Complete screen confirms what sign-in methods you enabled.
  6. Tap Done to finalize the procedure. The biometrics toggle is now on.

Disable biometrics

Note: If biometric verification is mandatory in your organization, you can’t disable this verification method.

  1. Open and tap your account.
  2. On the Account details page, go to the Options section and turn off Use biometrics.

  3. When prompted, verify your identity with biometrics.
  4. After confirming your identity, the biometrics verification option is disabled.
