Troubleshooting known issues on Android devices

Learn how to troubleshoot known issues that might occur on your Android device. If your problem persists, contact your organization’s help desk.

Can’t add an existing account to a new device

Issue

You already have an active account but can't add it to a new device.

Solution

Can’t add an account using a sign-in URL

Issue

OKTA-408394

You already have an account configured for signing in with Push Notification and . Biometrics are enabled in your account. If you add another account using the sign-in URL, prompts you to enter your username and to approve a push notification generated by your original account. After you provide biometrics, closes instead of adding the account.

Solution

Use a QR code to add an account on the same device.

When you launch the from , the wrong dashboard opens

Issue

OKTA-414897

If you access the or -protected apps in your mobile browser and then launch the dashboard from a different account, the dashboard from your previous visit might appear in the browser.

Solution

To ensure that you’re using the right dashboard, sign out and sign in again.

If is in your work and personal profile, you might not always be able to sign in with

Issue

OKTA-441512

After you enter your username and select Use , doesn’t show the “Tap to verify your identity with ” message. If you tap the Open button, you are still not prompted to verify your identity.

Solution

Use one of these options:

  • Select a different security method, such as Enter a code or Get a push notification.
  • Ensure that the account that you use to sign in with is the right one for the profile you work in. For example:
    • Account A is the account in your personal profile.
    • Account B is the account in your work profile.

    To sign in to your apps dashboard with Account B, open the browser in the work profile, enter the sign-in URL, and tap Sign in with .

When you sign in with in Firefox or Samsung Internet Browser, you are prompted for additional setup

Issue

OKTA-440031

If you tap Sign in with or select the Use security method to sign in to apps in Firefox or Samsung Internet Browser, you receive the message: Additional setup required to use .

Solution

Ignore the remediation instructions and select a different security method to sign in, such as Enter a code or Get a push notification.
